### <name>TELECOMMUNICATIONS (CARRIER LICENCE CHARGES) ACT 1997 - LONG TITLE </name>
</b>** 

## SECT
<sect>     An Act to impose charges in relation to carrier licences              under the Telecommunications Act 1997 </sect>
**<b>

### <name>TELECOMMUNICATIONS (CARRIER LICENCE CHARGES) ACT 1997 - PART 1  Part 1-Introduction </name>
</b>** 
**<b>

### <name>TELECOMMUNICATIONS (CARRIER LICENCE CHARGES) ACT 1997 - SECT 1 1 Short title </name>
</b>** 

## SECT
<sect>   This Act may be cited as the Telecommunications (Carrier Licence Charges) Act 1997\. </sect>
**<b>

### <name>TELECOMMUNICATIONS (CARRIER LICENCE CHARGES) ACT 1997 - SECT 2 2 Commencement </name>
</b>** 

## SECT
<sect>   (1) Subject to this section, this Act commences on the day on which it receives the Royal Assent. 

<lf>   (2) If the day on which this Act receives the Royal Assent is a later day than 5 June 1997, this Act is taken to have commenced on 5 June 1997\. </lf>
</sect>
**<b>

### <name>TELECOMMUNICATIONS (CARRIER LICENCE CHARGES) ACT 1997 - SECT 3 3 Act to bind Crown </name>
</b>** 

## SECT
<sect>   This Act binds the Crown in right of each of the States, of the Australian Capital Territory, of the Northern Territory and of Norfolk Island. </sect>
**<b>

### <name>TELECOMMUNICATIONS (CARRIER LICENCE CHARGES) ACT 1997 - SECT 4 4 Extension to eligible Territories </name>
</b>** 

## SECT
<sect>   This Act extends to each eligible Territory. </sect>
**<b>

### <name>TELECOMMUNICATIONS (CARRIER LICENCE CHARGES) ACT 1997 - SECT 5 5 Interpretation </name>
</b>** 

## SECT
<sect>   In this Act:   ACA means the Australian Communications Authority.   ACCC means the Australian Competition and Consumer Commission.   carrier has the same meaning as in the Telecommunications Act 1997\.   carrier licence has the same meaning as in the Telecommunications Act 1997\.   eligible Territory has the same meaning as in the Telecommunications Act 1997\. </sect>
**<b>

### <name>TELECOMMUNICATIONS (CARRIER LICENCE CHARGES) ACT 1997 - PART 2  Part 2-Application charge </name>
</b>** 
**<b>

### <name>TELECOMMUNICATIONS (CARRIER LICENCE CHARGES) ACT 1997 - SECT 6 6 Definition of charge </name>
</b>** 

## SECT
<sect>   In this Part:   charge means charge imposed by this Part. </sect>
**<b>

### <name>TELECOMMUNICATIONS (CARRIER LICENCE CHARGES) ACT 1997 - SECT 7 7 Imposition of charge </name>
</b>** 

## SECT
<sect>   Charge is imposed on an application for a carrier licence. </sect>
**<b>

### <name>TELECOMMUNICATIONS (CARRIER LICENCE CHARGES) ACT 1997 - SECT 8 8 By whom charge payable </name>
</b>** 

## SECT
<sect>   Charge imposed on an application for a carrier licence is payable by the applicant. </sect>
**<b>

### <name>TELECOMMUNICATIONS (CARRIER LICENCE CHARGES) ACT 1997 - SECT 9 9 Amount of charge </name>
</b>** 

## SECT
<sect>   (1) The amount of charge imposed on an application for a carrier licence is the amount ascertained in accordance with a written determination made by the ACA. 

<lf>   (2) A determination under subsection (1) is a disallowable instrument for the purposes of section 46A of the Acts Interpretation Act 1901\. </lf>
</sect>
**<b>

### <name>TELECOMMUNICATIONS (CARRIER LICENCE CHARGES) ACT 1997 - SECT 10 10 Maximum charge </name>
</b>** 

## SECT
<sect>   The amount of charge imposed on an application for a carrier licence must not exceed $100,000\. </sect>
**<b>

### <name>TELECOMMUNICATIONS (CARRIER LICENCE CHARGES) ACT 1997 - PART 3  Part 3-Annual charge </name>
</b>** 
**<b>

### <name>TELECOMMUNICATIONS (CARRIER LICENCE CHARGES) ACT 1997 - SECT 11 11 Definition of charge </name>
</b>** 

## SECT
<sect>   In this Part:   charge means charge imposed by this Part. </sect>
**<b>

### <name>TELECOMMUNICATIONS (CARRIER LICENCE CHARGES) ACT 1997 - SECT 12 12 Imposition of charge </name>
</b>** 

## SECT
<sect>   Charge is imposed on a carrier licence that is in force at the beginning of a financial year, where the financial year begins on or after 1 July 1998\. </sect>
**<b>

### <name>TELECOMMUNICATIONS (CARRIER LICENCE CHARGES) ACT 1997 - SECT 13 13 By whom charge payable </name>
</b>** 

## SECT
<sect>   Charge imposed on a carrier licence is payable by the holder of the licence. </sect>
**<b>

### <name>TELECOMMUNICATIONS (CARRIER LICENCE CHARGES) ACT 1997 - SECT 14 14 Amount of charge </name>
</b>** 

## SECT
<sect>   (1) The amount of charge imposed on a carrier licence is the amount ascertained in accordance with a written determination made by the ACA. 

<lf>   (2) A determination under subsection (1) is a disallowable instrument for the purposes of section 46A of the Acts Interpretation Act 1901\. </lf>
</sect>
**<b>

### <name>TELECOMMUNICATIONS (CARRIER LICENCE CHARGES) ACT 1997 - SECT 15 15 Limit on total of charges </name>
</b>** 

## SECT
<sect>   (1) The total of the charges that are imposed on carrier licences in force at the beginning of a financial year must not exceed the sum of:   (a) the amount determined, by a written instrument made by the ACA, to be the proportion of the ACA's costs for the immediately preceding financial year that is attributable to the ACA's telecommunications functions and powers; and   (b) the amount determined, by a written instrument made by the ACCC, to be the proportion of the ACCC's costs for the immediately preceding financial year that is attributable to the ACCC's telecommunications functions and powers; and   (c) the amount determined, by a written instrument made by the ACA, to be the proportion of the Commonwealth's contribution to the budget of the International Telecommunication Union for the calendar year in which the beginning of the financial year occurs that is to be recovered from carriers; and   (d) the amount determined, in a written instrument made by the Minister, to be the estimated total amount of grants likely to be made during the financial year under section 593 of the Telecommunications Act 1997\. 

<lf>   (2) An instrument under subsection (1) must be notified in the Gazette before the day on which the charges referred to in subsection (1) become due for payment. <p><lf>   (3) An instrument under subsection (1) is a disallowable instrument for the purposes of section 46A of the Acts Interpretation Act 1901\. <p><lf>   (4) In this section:   ACA's telecommunications functions has the same meaning as in the Australian Communications Authority Act 1997\.   ACA's telecommunications powers has the same meaning as in the Telecommunications Act 1997\.   ACCC's telecommunications functions and powers has the same meaning as in the Telecommunications Act 1997\.   cost:   (a) in relation to the ACA, means an amount that, in accordance with accrual-based accounting principles, is treated as a cost of the ACA; and   (b) in relation to the ACCC, means an amount that, in accordance with accrual-based accounting principles, is treated as a cost of the ACCC. </lf></p></lf></p></lf>
</sect>
