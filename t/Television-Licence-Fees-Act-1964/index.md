### <name>TELEVISION LICENCE FEES ACT 1964 - LONG TITLE </name>
</b>** 

## SECT
<sect>       An Act to provide for the payment of fees in respect of<lf>                     certain television licences<lf> </lf></lf></sect>
**<b>

### <name>TELEVISION LICENCE FEES ACT 1964 - SECT 1 Short title </name>
</b>** 

## SECT
<sect>   1\. This Act may be cited as the Television Licence Fees Act 1964.*1* SEE NOTES TO FIRST ARTICLE OF THIS CHAPTER . </sect>
**<b>

### <name>TELEVISION LICENCE FEES ACT 1964 - SECT 2 Commencement </name>
</b>** 

## SECT
<sect>   2\. This Act shall come into operation on the day on which it receives the Royal Assent.*1* SEE NOTES TO FIRST ARTICLE OF THIS CHAPTER . </sect>
**<b>

### <name>TELEVISION LICENCE FEES ACT 1964 - SECT 3 Incorporation of Broadcasting Act </name>
</b>** 

## SECT
<sect>   3\. The Broadcasting Act 1942 is incorporated and shall be read as one with this Act. </sect>
**<b>

### <name>TELEVISION LICENCE FEES ACT 1964 - SECT 4 Interpretation </name>
</b>** 

## SECT
<sect>   4.(1) In this Act-<lf>   'gross earnings', in respect of a licence in respect of a period, means-<lf>          (a) in a case to which paragraph (b) does not apply-the gross earnings<lf> of the licensee during that period from the televising, pursuant to the licence, of advertisements or other matter and from the televising, pursuant to any MCS permit granted in respect of the licence, of advertisements or other matter; and<lf>          (b) in the case of a remote licence to which sub-section<lf> 81(6) of the Broadcasting Act 1942 applies-the aggregate amount of the gross earnings during that period from the televising, pursuant to the licence, of advertisements or other matter of all the persons who, at any time during that period, are co-owners of the licence;<lf>   'licence' means a commercial television licence, a remote television licence or a supplementary television licence; (2) Where, in connexion with any transaction, any consideration is paid or given otherwise than in cash, the money value of that consideration shall, for the purposes of this Act, be deemed to have been paid or given. </lf></lf></lf></lf></lf></lf></sect>
**<b>

### <name>TELEVISION LICENCE FEES ACT 1964 - SECT 5 Licence fees </name>
</b>** 

## SECT
<sect>   5.(1) Subject to sub-section (2), there is payable to the Commonwealth by a licensee, by way of tax in respect of his licence, fees in accordance with sections 6 and 6A.<lf>   (2) The regulations may make provision for rebates of fees payable by licensees. </lf></sect>
**<b>

### <name>TELEVISION LICENCE FEES ACT 1964 - SECT 6 Amount of fees </name>
</b>** 

## SECT
<sect>   6.(1) There is payable on the grant of a licence, not being a grant by way of the renewal of a licence, a fee of $500.<lf>   (2) Subject to section 6A, there is payable on each anniversary of ### <name>TELEVISION LICENCE FEES ACT 1964 - SECT 7 Earnings of subsidiary companies etc. </name>
</b>** 

## SECT
<sect>   7\. Where the Minister is of the opinion that-<lf>   (a) an amount, or part of an amount, earned during any period by a person other than a licensee would, if the licensee and that person were the same person, form part of the gross earnings in respect of the licence in respect of that period for the purposes of this Act; and<lf>    (b) a relationship exists between the licensee and the other person (whether<lf> by reason of any shareholding or of any agreement or arrangement, or for any other reason) of such a kind that the amount or the part of the amount, as the case may be, should, for the purposes of this Act, be treated as part of the gross earnings in respect of the licence in respect of that period, the Minister may direct that the amount or the part of the amount, as the case may be, shall be so treated. </lf></lf></lf></sect>

### <name>TELEVISION LICENCE FEES ACT 1964 - SECT 8 Regulations </name>

## SECT

The Governor-General may make regulations, not inconsistent with this Act, prescribing all matters-<lf>    (a) required or permitted by this Act to be prescribed; or<lf>    (b) necessary or<lf> convenient to be prescribed in carrying out or giving effect to this Act. </lf></lf></lf></sect>
