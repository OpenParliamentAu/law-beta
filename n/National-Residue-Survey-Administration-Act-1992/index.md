###  NATIONAL RESIDUE SURVEY ADMINISTRATION ACT 1992<part>Part&#160;1&#151;Preliminary </part>
**
###  NATIONAL RESIDUE SURVEY ADMINISTRATION ACT 1992- SECT 1  Short title [_see_ Note 1] 
<dl compact="">

		This Act may be cited as the _National Residue Survey Administration Act 1992_.

 </dl>
###  NATIONAL RESIDUE SURVEY ADMINISTRATION ACT 1992- SECT 2  Commencement 
<dl compact="">

		This Act commences on 1&#160;July 1993.

 </dl>
###  NATIONAL RESIDUE SURVEY ADMINISTRATION ACT 1992- SECT 3  Act binds Crown 
<dl compact="">

		This Act binds the Crown in right of each of the States, of the Australian

Capital Territory, of the Northern Territory and of Norfolk Island.

 </dl>

###  NATIONAL RESIDUE SURVEY ADMINISTRATION ACT 1992<part>Part&#160;2&#151;Interpretation </part>
**
###  NATIONAL RESIDUE SURVEY ADMINISTRATION ACT 1992- SECT 4  Interpretation 
<dl compact="">

		In this Act:

 </dl>
<dl compact=""><dl compact="">

_applicable products_ means:

 </dl></dl>

<dl compact=""><dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(a)</dt><dd>food products; or</dd>

<dt>(b)</dt><dd>products of a primary industry; or</dd>

<dt>(c)</dt><dd>any other products that are of a kind used as inputs to the production of

products referred to in paragraph&#160;(a) or (b);

</dd>

</dl></dl></dl>

whether or not the products have been subjected to any process.

<def><dl compact=""><dl compact="">

_National Residue Survey_ _Levy_ means levy of that

name payable under:

 </dl></dl>

<dl compact=""><dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(a)</dt><dd>the _National Residue Survey (Customs) Levy Act 1998_; or</dd>

<dt>(b)</dt><dd>the _National Residue Survey (Excise) Levy Act 1998_.

</dd>

</dl></dl></dl>

<def><dl compact=""><dl compact="">

_process_, in relation to a product, means the performance of any

operation in relation to the product, and includes, but is not limited to, the

harvesting, chilling, freezing, drying, bottling, packing, canning or

preserving of the product. _products of a primary industry_ means products that result from any of the following:  </dl></dl>

<center>Back to Top</center>

<dl compact=""><dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(a)</dt><dd>agriculture or the cultivation of land;</dd>

<dt>(b)</dt><dd>the maintenance of animals for commercial purposes;</dd>

<dt>(c)</dt><dd>fishing or aquaculture;</dd>

<dt>(d)</dt><dd>hunting or trapping;</dd>

<dt>(e)</dt><dd>horticulture;</dd>

<dt>(f)</dt><dd>any other primary industry activity.

</dd>

</dl></dl></dl>

<def><dl compact=""><dl compact="">

_Reserve_ means the National Residue Survey Reserve established by

section&#160;6\. _Secretary_ means Secretary of the Department.  </dl></dl>

###  NATIONAL RESIDUE SURVEY ADMINISTRATION ACT 1992- SECT 6  Establishment of Reserve 
**

 <dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(1)</dt><dd>This subsection establishes a reserve called the National Residue Survey

Reserve.</dd> <dt>(2)</dt><dd>The Reserve is a component of the Reserved Money Fund. </dd> </dl></dl>

**

###  NATIONAL RESIDUE SURVEY ADMINISTRATION ACT 1992- SECT 7  Payments into National Residue Survey Reserve 
**

 <dl compact=""><dl compact="">

		There must be transferred to the Reserve from the Consolidated Revenue Fund:

 </dl></dl>

<dl compact=""><dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(a)</dt><dd>in respect of each amount of National Residue Survey Levy collected by or

on behalf of the Commonwealth&#151;an amount equal to that amount of levy; and</dd>

<dt>(b)</dt><dd>in respect of each amount of penalty collected by or on behalf of the

Commonwealth in connection with National Residue Survey Levy&#151;an amount equal

to that amount of penalty; and</dd>

<dt>(c)</dt><dd>amounts equal to amounts paid to the Commonwealth for the purposes of the

National Residue Survey or the Reserve;</dd>

<dt>(e)</dt><dd>amounts appropriated by the Parliament for the purposes of the National

Residue Survey or the Reserve; and</dd>

<dt>(f)</dt><dd>amounts equal to income from the investment of money from the Reserve.

</dd>

</dl></dl></dl>

**

###  NATIONAL RESIDUE SURVEY ADMINISTRATION ACT 1992- SECT 8  Payments out of Reserve 
**

 <dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(1)</dt><dd>Amounts standing to the credit of the Reserve may be spent in:

</dd> </dl></dl>

<dl compact=""><dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(a)</dt><dd>making payments, in accordance with an expenditure program approved by the

Minister, for purposes related to:

</dd>

</dl></dl></dl>

<dl compact=""><dl compact=""><dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(i)</dt><dd>the monitoring and reporting of the level of contaminants in applicable

products; and</dd>

<dt>(ii)</dt><dd>if the relevant body (as determined by the Minister) in the industry

concerned agrees to the program extending to expenditure under this

subparagraph&#151;the testing, either on a random basis or in specific cases, of

applicable products or the environment for the purpose of tracing the sources

of contaminants and determining the causes of contamination; and</dd>

<dt>(iii)</dt><dd>if the relevant body (as determined by the Minister) in the industry

concerned agrees to the program extending to expenditure under this

subparagraph&#151;the testing and reporting of the level of contaminants in

applicable products or the environment, and any associated activities, for the

purpose of investigating the potential sources, and determining the potential

causes, of such contaminants; and</dd>

<dt>(iv)</dt><dd>if the relevant body (as determined by the Minister) in the industry

concerned agrees to the program extending to expenditure under this

subparagraph&#151;the prevention of contamination in, and the management of risks

associated with, contamination of applicable products; and

</dd>

</dl></dl></dl></dl>

<dl compact=""><dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(b)</dt><dd>making payments to the Commonwealth of amounts equal to expenditure

incurred by the Commonwealth in relation to:

</dd>

</dl></dl></dl>

<center>Back to Top</center>

<dl compact=""><dl compact=""><dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(i)</dt><dd>the collection and recovery of amounts referred to in paragraph 7(a) or

(b); or</dd>

<dt>(ii)</dt><dd>the administration of paragraph 7(a) or (b); or </dd>

<dt>(iii)</dt><dd>the carrying out of any activities of kinds referred to in

paragraph&#160;(a) or any related activities; and

</dd>

</dl></dl></dl></dl>

<dl compact=""><dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(c)</dt><dd>if an amount that was paid into the Reserve was given by a person or body

to the Commonwealth for a particular purpose but has not been fully spent for

that </dd>

purpose&#151;repaying the unspent amount to the person or body; and

<dt>(d)</dt><dd>if an amount that was paid into the Reserve was appropriated by the

Parliament for the purposes of the Reserve on condition that the amount be

repaid&#151;repaying the amount.

</dd>

</dl></dl></dl>

<dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(2)</dt><dd>Without limiting the generality of subsection&#160;(1), the reference in

that subsection to expenditure incurred includes a reference to:

</dd> </dl></dl>

<dl compact=""><dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(a)</dt><dd>remuneration and allowances paid to staff; and</dd>

<dt>(b)</dt><dd>other expenditure incurred in relation to the provision of staff or the

provision of the services of staff; and</dd>

<dt>(c)</dt><dd>expenditure incurred in relation to surveys conducted for the purpose of

monitoring the level of contaminants; and</dd>

<dt>(d)</dt><dd>expenditure incurred in relation to the provision of accommodation or

administrative support.

</dd>

</dl></dl></dl>

###  NATIONAL RESIDUE SURVEY ADMINISTRATION ACT 1992- SECT 10  Report by Minister 
**

 <dl compact=""><dl compact="">

		As soon as practicable after the end of each financial year, the Minister is

to cause a report to be laid before each House of the Parliament setting out

details of the operation of the Reserve including:

 </dl></dl>

<dl compact=""><dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(a)</dt><dd>money paid into the Reserve during that financial year; and</dd>

<dt>(b)</dt><dd>money paid out of the Reserve during that financial year; and</dd>

<dt>(c)</dt><dd>financial statements relating to the Reserve for that financial year;

and</dd>

<dt>(d)</dt><dd>a description of activities undertaken in relation to the National Residue

Survey during that financial year.

</dd>

</dl></dl></dl>

**

###  NATIONAL RESIDUE SURVEY ADMINISTRATION ACT 1992- SECT 11  Release of information 
**

 <dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(1)</dt><dd>Subject to subsection&#160;(2), information collected under the activities

referred to in section&#160;8 using funds paid or reimbursed out of the Reserve

that identifies a particular person may not be released to any other person.</dd> <dt>(2)</dt><dd>Information of a kind mentioned in subsection&#160;(1) may be released: </dd> </dl></dl>

<dl compact=""><dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(a)</dt><dd>to authorities of the Commonwealth, of a State or of a Territory that are

responsible for the monitoring or regulation of agricultural or veterinary

chemical residues and contaminants in applicable products or the environment

for the purpose of such monitoring or regulation or the management of

associated risks; and</dd>

<dt>(b)</dt><dd>to a person whom the Secretary has approved in writing to be an

appropriate person to be granted access to the information.

</dd>

</dl></dl></dl>

<dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(3)</dt><dd>The Secretary may approve a person to be an appropriate person for the

purposes of paragraph (2)(b) only if the Secretary is satisfied that the person

requires the relevant information to enable the person to monitor, regulate or

manage agricultural or veterinary chemical residues or contaminants in

applicable products or the environment.</dd> <dt>(4)</dt><dd>An approval is to specify: </dd> </dl></dl>

<center>Back to Top</center>

<dl compact=""><dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(a)</dt><dd>each person who may be granted access to the relevant information under

paragraph&#160;(2)(b); and</dd>

<dt>(b)</dt><dd>the purpose or purposes for which the information can be used.

</dd>

</dl></dl></dl>

<dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(5)</dt><dd>A person to whom information is released under paragraph&#160;(2)(b)

commits an offence if the information is used otherwise than for a purpose

specified in the approval.

</dd> </dl></dl>

Penalty:	Imprisonment for 12 months.

<dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(6)</dt><dd>This section does not affect the operation of section&#160;27 of the

_Primary Industries Levies and Charges Collection Act 1991_.</dd> <dt>(7)</dt><dd>The Secretary may, in writing, delegate to an APS employee in the Department the power to give approvals under paragraph&#160;(2)(b).</dd> <dt>(8)</dt><dd>A delegate is, in the exercise of a delegated power, subject to the directions of the Secretary. </dd> </dl></dl>

</dd>
