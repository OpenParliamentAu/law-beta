###  MEDICAL INDEMNITY (UMP SUPPORT PAYMENT) ACT 2002- SECT 1  Short title [_see_ Note 1] 
<dl compact=""> 		This Act may be cited as the _Medical Indemnity (UMP Support Payment) Act 2002_. 

</dl>
###  MEDICAL INDEMNITY (UMP SUPPORT PAYMENT) ACT 2002- SECT 2  Commencement 
<dl compact=""> 		This Act commences, or is taken to have commenced, on 1&#160;January 2003\. 

</dl>
###  MEDICAL INDEMNITY (UMP SUPPORT PAYMENT) ACT 2002- SECT 3  Definitions 
<dl compact=""> <dt>(1)</dt><dd>In this Act, unless the contrary intention appears: </dd>

</dl>
<dl compact=""><dl compact=""> _annual subscription_ for a participating member of a participating MDO has the same meaning as in section&#160;54 of the _Medical Indemnity Act 2002_.

 _base year_ for a participating member of a participating MDO has the meaning given by section&#160;54 of the _Medical Indemnity Act 2002_.

 _contribution year_ has the meaning given by subsections 5(1) and (2).

 _gross medical income_, of a health professional for a period, means the sum of the amounts payable in respect of health care related services rendered by, or on behalf of, the health professional during the period.

 _gross Medicare billable income_, of a medical practitioner for a period, means the sum of the medical expenses payable in respect of professional services: 

</dl></dl> <dl compact=""><dl compact=""><dl compact=""> <dt>(a)</dt><dd>that were rendered by, or on behalf of, the medical practitioner during the period; and</dd> <dt>(b)</dt><dd>for which medicare benefits were or are payable under Part&#160;II of the _Health Insurance Act 1973_. </dd> </dl></dl></dl> <def><dl compact=""><dl compact=""> _health professional_ has the same meaning as in the _Medical Indemnity Act 2002_.

 _imposition day_ has the meaning given by subsection 5(3).

 _income period_, for a contribution  year, means: 

</dl></dl> <dl compact=""><dl compact=""><dl compact=""> <dt>(a)</dt><dd>the period, of the length specified in the regulations, ending on the day, in that year, specified in the regulations; or</dd> <dt>(b)</dt><dd>if the regulations do not specify a period&#151;the period of 12 months ending on 30&#160;September in that year. </dd> </dl></dl></dl> <def><dl compact=""><dl compact=""> _medical expenses_ has the same meaning as in the _Health Insurance Act 1973_.

 _medical practitioner_ has the same meaning as in the _Medical Indemnity Act 2002_.

 _participating MDO_ has the same meaning as in the _Medical Indemnity Act 2002_.

 _participating member_ of a participating MDO has the same meaning as in the _Medical Indemnity Act 2002_.

 _professional service_ has the same meaning as in the _Health Insurance Act 1973_.

 _UMP support payment_ means the payment that is payable under Division&#160;1 of Part&#160;3 of the _Medical Indemnity Act 2002_. 

</dl></dl>

</dl></dl> <dl compact=""><dl compact=""><dl compact=""> <dt>(a)</dt><dd>the amount payable in respect of a health care related service; or</dd> <dt>(b)</dt><dd>the medical expenses payable in respect of a professional service; </dd> </dl></dl></dl>

###  MEDICAL INDEMNITY (UMP SUPPORT PAYMENT) ACT 2002- SECT 4  Imposition of UMP support payment 
**  <dl compact=""><dl compact=""> <dt>(1)</dt><dd>On the imposition day for each contribution year for a participating MDO, a UMP support payment is imposed as a tax on the participating members of the MDO.</dd>

 <dt>(2)</dt><dd>If there is an imposition day, for a contribution year for a participating MDO, for a particular class of participating members of the MDO, the reference in subsection&#160;(1) to the imposition day for that year for the MDO is taken, in its application to a member of that class, to be a reference to that imposition day for that class. </dd>

</dl></dl>  **

###  MEDICAL INDEMNITY (UMP SUPPORT PAYMENT) ACT 2002- SECT 5  Contribution years and imposition days 
**  <dl compact=""><dl compact=""> <dt>(1)</dt><dd>Subject to subsection&#160;(2), each financial year that starts on or after 1&#160;July 2003 is a _contribution year_ for a participating MDO.</dd>

 <dt>(2)</dt><dd>The regulations may declare that a financial year specified in the regulations is the last contribution year for a particular participating MDO. If they do so, no subsequent financial year is a contribution year for that MDO.</dd>

 <dt>(3)</dt><dd>The _imposition day_ for a contribution year for a participating MDO is: </dd>

</dl></dl> <dl compact=""><dl compact=""><dl compact=""> <dt>(a)</dt><dd>for the contribution year that starts on 1&#160;July 2003: </dd> </dl></dl></dl> 

	(i)	1&#160;March 2004; or
 	(ii)	such other day as is specified in the regulations as the imposition day for the contribution year; and
 <dl compact=""><dl compact=""><dl compact=""> <dt>(b)</dt><dd>for a later contribution year: </dd> </dl></dl></dl> 

	(i)	1&#160;May in the contribution year; or
 	(ii)	such other day as is specified in the regulations as the imposition day for the contribution year.
 <dl compact=""><dl compact=""> <dt>(4)</dt><dd>Regulations made for the purposes of subparagraph&#160;(3)(a)(ii) or (3)(b)(ii) may specify different imposition days, for a contribution year for a participating MDO, for different classes of participating members of the MDO. </dd>

</dl></dl>  **

###  MEDICAL INDEMNITY (UMP SUPPORT PAYMENT) ACT 2002- SECT 6  Amount of UMP support payment 
**  <dl compact=""><dl compact=""> <dt>(1A)</dt><dd>The amount of the UMP support payment imposed on a participating member of a participating MDO for the contribution year for the MDO that starts on 1&#160;July 2003 is the least of: </dd>

</dl></dl> <dl compact=""><dl compact=""><dl compact=""> <dt>(a)</dt><dd>the applicable percentage of the member's annual subscription for the base year; and</dd> <dt>(b)</dt><dd>$1,000; and</dd> <dt>(c)</dt><dd>if the member is a medical practitioner during the income period for that contribution  year&#151;2% of the member's gross Medicare billable income for the income period for that contribution  year; and</dd> <dt>(d)</dt><dd>if the member is a health professional during, but is not a medical practitioner at any time during, the income period for that contribution  year&#151;2% of the member's gross medical income for the income period for that contribution  year. </dd> </dl></dl></dl> 
<center>Back to Top</center>
 <dl compact=""><dl compact=""> <dt>(1B)</dt><dd>The amount of the UMP support payment imposed on a participating member of a participating MDO for the contribution year for the MDO that starts on 1&#160;July 2004 is the amount worked out by adding: </dd>

</dl></dl> <dl compact=""><dl compact=""><dl compact=""> <dt>(a)</dt><dd>the least of: </dd> </dl></dl></dl> 

	(i)	$2,500; and
 	(ii)	half of the applicable percentage of the member's annual subscription for the base year; and
 	(iii)	if the member is a medical practitioner during the income period for that contribution  year&#151;1% of the member's gross Medicare billable income for the income period for that contribution  year; and
 	(iv)	if the member is a health professional during, but is not a medical practitioner at any time during, the income period for that contribution  year&#151;1% of the member's gross medical income for the income period for that contribution  year; and
 <dl compact=""><dl compact=""><dl compact=""> <dt>(b)</dt><dd>the least of: </dd> </dl></dl></dl> 

	(i)	$500; and
 	(ii)	half of the applicable percentage of the member's annual subscription for the base year; and
 	(iii)	if the member is a medical practitioner during the income period for that contribution  year&#151;1% of the member's gross Medicare billable income for the income period for that contribution  year; and
 	(iv)	if the member is a health professional during, but is not a medical practitioner at any time during, the income period for that contribution  year&#151;1% of the member's gross medical income for the income period for that contribution  year.
 <dl compact=""><dl compact=""> <dt>(1)</dt><dd>The amount of the UMP support payment imposed on a participating member of a participating MDO for a contribution  year for the MDO that starts on or after 1&#160;July 2005 is the least of: </dd>

</dl></dl> <dl compact=""><dl compact=""><dl compact=""> <dt>(a)</dt><dd>$5,000; and</dd> <dt>(b)</dt><dd>the applicable percentage of the member's annual subscription for the base year; and</dd> <dt>(c)</dt><dd>if the member is a medical practitioner during the income period for that contribution  year&#151;2% of the member's gross Medicare billable income for the income period for that contribution  year; and</dd> <dt>(d)</dt><dd>if the member is a health professional during, but is not a medical practitioner at any time during, the income period for that contribution  year&#151;2% of the member's gross medical income for the income period for that contribution  year. </dd> </dl></dl></dl> <dl compact=""><dl compact=""> <dt>(2)</dt><dd>The applicable percentage is: </dd>

</dl></dl> <dl compact=""><dl compact=""><dl compact=""> <dt>(a)</dt><dd>50%; or</dd> <dt>(b)</dt><dd>such lower percentage as is specified in the regulations in relation to the MDO for the contribution year. </dd> </dl></dl></dl> <dl compact=""><dl compact=""> <dt>(3)</dt><dd>Without limiting paragraph&#160;(2)(b), regulations made for the purposes of that paragraph may specify different percentages for different contribution years for the MDO.</dd>

 <dt>(4)</dt><dd>The regulations made for the purposes of paragraph&#160;(2)(b) may not specify a percentage for a contribution year for an MDO (other than the first contribution year) that is higher than the percentage applicable to the MDO for the first contribution year. </dd>

</dl></dl>  **

_Medical Indemnity (IBNR Indemnity) Contribution Amendment Act 2004_(No.&#160;18, 2004)
 **Schedule 1**

 **24  Renaming of the IBNR indemnity contribution does not affect existing liabilities** 

To avoid doubt, the amendments made by this Act and the _Medical Indemnity Amendment Act 2004_ to change the name of the IBNR indemnity contribution do not affect a person's liability to pay the IBNR indemnity contribution.
 **25  References in regulations or other instruments to the IBNR indemnity contribution** 

A reference in a regulation or other instrument made under the _Medical Indemnity Act 2002_ or the _Medical Indemnity (IBNR Indemnity) Contribution Act 2002_ to the IBNR indemnity contribution is taken, after the commencement of this Schedule, to be a reference to the UMP support payment.
