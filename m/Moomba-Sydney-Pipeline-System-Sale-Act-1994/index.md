<sect>   3\. (1) In this Act, unless the contrary intention appears:<lf>   "AGL" means The Australian Gas Light Company;<lf>   "asset" means:<lf>   (a) any legal or equitable estate or interest in real or personal property, including a contingent or prospective one;
