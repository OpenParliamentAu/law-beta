###  MOOMBA-SYDNEY PIPELINE SYSTEM SALE ACT 1994- SECT 1  Short title [_see_ Note 1] 
<dl compact="">

		This Act may be cited as the _Moomba-Sydney Pipeline System Sale Act 1994_.

 </dl>
###  MOOMBA-SYDNEY PIPELINE SYSTEM SALE ACT 1994- SECT 2  Commencement [_see_ Note 1] 
<dl compact="">

<dt>(1)</dt><dd>Sections 1, 2, 3, 4, 6 and 7 commence on the day on which this Act

receives the Royal Assent.</dd> <dt>(2)</dt><dd>Subject to subsection (3), the remaining provisions of this Act commence on the sale day.</dd> <dt>(3)</dt><dd>If: </dd> </dl>
<dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(a)</dt><dd>the Commonwealth and Newco do not enter into an agreement on the sale day

for the sale of the Moomba-Sydney pipeline system by the Commonwealth; or</dd>

<dt>(b)</dt><dd>if the agreement is entered into but Newco does not pay the Commonwealth

the amount it is required to pay the Commonwealth on the sale day under the

agreement;

</dd>

</dl></dl>

<dl compact=""><dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(b)</dt><dd>any right, privilege or immunity, including a contingent or prospective

<dl compact=""><dl compact=""><dl compact="">

###  MOOMBA-SYDNEY PIPELINE SYSTEM SALE ACT 1994- SECT 7  Staff member of the Authority may consent to being employed by Newco 
**

<dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(1)</dt><dd>This section applies to each staff member of the Authority who receives a

Newco employment offer at least 15 days before the sale day. For this purpose,

_Newco employment offer_ means an offer of employment with Newco

beginning on the sale day that is made by AGL on its own behalf and on behalf

of other prospective buyers of shares in Newco.</dd> <dt>(2)</dt><dd>The staff member may consent to being employed by Newco by notice in writing given to the Chief Executive Officer of the Authority within 2 weeks after the staff member receives the offer of employment.</dd> <dt>(3)</dt><dd>The staff member may revoke his or her consent at any time before the sale day by notice in writing given to the Chief Executive Officer of the Authority. </dd> </dl></dl>

**

###  MOOMBA-SYDNEY PIPELINE SYSTEM SALE ACT 1994- SECT 8  Certain staff members of the Authority to become employees of Newco 
**

<dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(1)</dt><dd>This section applies to each staff member of the Authority who:

</dd> </dl></dl>

<dl compact=""><dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(a)</dt><dd>has consented to being employed by Newco under section 7; and</dd>

<dt>(b)</dt><dd>has not revoked his or her consent before the sale day; and</dd>

<dt>(c)</dt><dd>was a staff member of the Authority immediately before the sale day.

</dd>

</dl></dl></dl>

<dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(2)</dt><dd>On the sale day, the staff member:

</dd> </dl></dl>

<dl compact=""><dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(a)</dt><dd>ceases, by force of this section, to be an officer or employee of the

Authority; and</dd>

<dt>(b)</dt><dd>becomes an employee of Newco, by force of this section, on the same terms

and conditions that applied to the staff member immediately before the sale day

because of an award.

</dd>

</dl></dl></dl>

<dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(3)</dt><dd>This section has effect subject to sections 9 and 10.

</dd> </dl></dl>

**

###  MOOMBA-SYDNEY PIPELINE SYSTEM SALE ACT 1994- SECT 9  Subdivision not to affect certain matters relating to transferring staff members 
**

<dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(1)</dt><dd>On and after the sale day, this section has effect for the purposes of

applying a law or award to Newco's employment of a transferring staff member.</dd> <dt>(2)</dt><dd>The service of the transferring staff member as an employee of Newco is to be regarded for all purposes as having been continuous with his or her service, immediately before the sale day, as an officer or employee of the Authority.</dd> <dt>(3)</dt><dd>The transferring staff member is to be regarded, on the sale day, as having: </dd> </dl></dl>

<center>Back to Top</center>

<dl compact=""><dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(a)</dt><dd>accrued an entitlement to Long Service Leave Act benefits, recreation

leave benefits and sickness leave benefits; and</dd>

<dt>(b)</dt><dd>an entitlement to recognition by Newco of years of service in connection

with that employment;

</dd>

</dl></dl></dl>

that is equivalent to the entitlement that the person had as an officer or employee of the Authority, immediately before the sale day.

<dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(4)</dt><dd>The transferring staff member is not entitled to receive any payment or

other benefit by reason only of having ceased to be an officer or employee of

the Authority because of this Act.

</dd> </dl></dl>

###  MOOMBA-SYDNEY PIPELINE SYSTEM SALE ACT 1994- SECT 12  Certain assets and liabilities of the Authority and Pacenco to become assets and liabilities of Newco 
**

<dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(1)</dt><dd>By force of this section, all assets of the Authority or Pacenco specified

in Schedule 1 to the asset purchase agreement cease to be assets of the

Authority or Pacenco and become assets of Newco on the sale day.</dd> <dt>(2)</dt><dd>By force of this section, all liabilities of the Authority or Pacenco specified in Schedule 2 to the asset purchase agreement cease to be liabilities of the Authority or Pacenco and become liabilities of Newco on the sale day. </dd> </dl></dl>

**

###  MOOMBA-SYDNEY PIPELINE SYSTEM SALE ACT 1994- SECT 13  References in certain instruments to the Authority or Pacenco to be references to Newco on and after the sale day 
**

<dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(1)</dt><dd>An Authority instrument continues to have effect on and after the sale day

as if a reference in the instrument to the Authority were a reference to

Newco.</dd> <dt>(2)</dt><dd>A Pacenco instrument continues to have effect on and after the sale day as if a reference in the instrument to Pacenco were a reference to Newco. </dd> </dl></dl>

###  MOOMBA-SYDNEY PIPELINE SYSTEM SALE ACT 1994- SECT 14  Dual destination interests become assets of Newco and the Commonwealth 
**

<dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(1)</dt><dd>By force of this section, all dual destination interests:

</dd> </dl></dl>

<dl compact=""><dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(a)</dt><dd>cease to be assets of the Authority or Pacenco; and</dd>

<dt>(b)</dt><dd>become assets in which Newco and the Commonwealth each have a half

interest as tenants in common;

</dd>

</dl></dl></dl>

on the sale day.

<dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(2)</dt><dd>Newco is to hold all jointly transferring interests on the terms and

conditions that apply to the interests under the easement agreement.

</dd> </dl></dl>

**

###  MOOMBA-SYDNEY PIPELINE SYSTEM SALE ACT 1994- SECT 15  Dual destination instruments that do not create easements 
**

<dl compact=""><dl compact="">

		A dual destination instrument that does not create an easement continues to

have effect, on and after the sale day, as if a reference in the instrument to

the Authority or Pacenco were a reference to Newco and the Commonwealth jointly.

 </dl></dl>

**

###  MOOMBA-SYDNEY PIPELINE SYSTEM SALE ACT 1994- SECT 16  Dual destination instruments that create easements 
**

 <dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(1)</dt><dd>This section applies to a dual destination instrument that creates an

easement in favour of the Authority or Pacenco.</dd> <dt>(2)</dt><dd>The dual destination instrument continues in effect subject to subsections <dt>(3)</dt><dd>and (4).</dd></dd> <dt>(3)</dt><dd>On and after the sale day, the dual destination instrument has effect as if the easement were an easement in favour of Newco and the Commonwealth jointly that allows Newco and the Commonwealth jointly, Newco or the Commonwealth at any time to: </dd> </dl></dl>

<dl compact=""><dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(a)</dt><dd>construct, maintain or operate pipelines or parts of pipelines that are

owned by Newco, the Commonwealth or any other person; and</dd>

<dt>(b)</dt><dd>licence or otherwise permit any other person to construct, maintain or

operate pipelines or parts of pipelines that are owned by any person;

</dd>

</dl></dl></dl>

that are to be used, or are used, for the carriage of petroleum, in trade or commerce, between a point in a State and a point in another State or Territory.

<dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(4)</dt><dd>Any easement rights created by the dual destination instrument are taken

to be rights of Newco and the Commonwealth jointly.</dd> <dt>(5)</dt><dd>This section does not limit the power of Newco and the Commonwealth to enter into an agreement with each other that governs the exercise of any rights by Newco or the Commonwealth under a jointly owned easement.</dd> <dt>(6)</dt><dd>This section is not intended to affect the operation of any law of a State or Territory with respect to the construction or operation of pipelines or parts of pipelines that is capable of operating concurrently with this section. </dd> </dl></dl>

###  MOOMBA-SYDNEY PIPELINE SYSTEM SALE ACT 1994- SECT 19  Commonwealth-guaranteed debts of the Authority become debts of the Commonwealth 
**

<dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(1)</dt><dd>By force of this section, all Commonwealth-guaranteed debts of the

Authority cease to be obligations of the Authority and become obligations of

the Commonwealth on the sale day.</dd> <dt>(2)</dt><dd>The Treasurer may authorise the payment of money to discharge the Commonwealth-guaranteed debts that become obligations of the Commonwealth, whether by ending those obligations or otherwise.</dd> <dt>(3)</dt><dd>The Consolidated Revenue Fund is appropriated for payments under this section. </dd> </dl></dl>

**

###  MOOMBA-SYDNEY PIPELINE SYSTEM SALE ACT 1994- SECT 20  Instruments relating to Commonwealth-guaranteed debts 
**

 <dl compact=""><dl compact="">

		An instrument relating to a Commonwealth-guaranteed debt of the Authority

continues to have effect, on and after the sale day, as if a reference in the

instrument to the Authority were a reference to the Commonwealth.

 </dl></dl>

**

###  MOOMBA-SYDNEY PIPELINE SYSTEM SALE ACT 1994- SECT 21  Commonwealth and Authority liable to pay certain amounts in relation to Commonwealth-guaranteed debts 
**

<dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(1)</dt><dd>On the sale day:

</dd> </dl></dl>

<dl compact=""><dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(a)</dt><dd>the Commonwealth becomes liable to pay to the Authority an amount equal to

the Commonwealth-guaranteed debts of the Authority on the sale day; and</dd>

<dt>(b)</dt><dd>the Authority becomes liable to pay to the Commonwealth an amount equal to

the Commonwealth-guaranteed debts of the Authority on that day.

</dd>

</dl></dl></dl>

<dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(2)</dt><dd>The amount payable under paragraph (1)(a) is to be set-off against the

amount payable under paragraph (1)(b).</dd> <dt>(3)</dt><dd>The Consolidated Revenue Fund is appropriated for payments under paragraph (1)(a). </dd> </dl></dl>

**

###  MOOMBA-SYDNEY PIPELINE SYSTEM SALE ACT 1994- SECT 22  Commonwealth liable to pay an amount to the Authority equal to Authority's debts to the Commonwealth and set-off of debts 
**

<dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(1)</dt><dd>On the sale day the Commonwealth becomes liable to pay to the Authority an

amount equal to the Authority's debt to the Commonwealth on that day.</dd> <dt>(2)</dt><dd>The amount payable under subsection (1) is to be set-off against the Authority's debt to the Commonwealth.</dd> <dt>(3)</dt><dd>The Consolidated Revenue Fund is appropriated for payments under this section. </dd> </dl></dl>

	(1)	If:
