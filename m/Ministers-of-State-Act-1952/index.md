###  Compilation Information

###  Long Title 
**

###  MINISTERS OF STATE ACT 1952- SECT 1  Short title [_see_ Note 1] 
This Act may be cited as the _Ministers of State Act 1952_.

 
###  MINISTERS OF STATE ACT 1952- SECT 2  Commencement 
This Act shall be deemed to have come into operation on 1st&#160;January, 1952.

 
###  MINISTERS OF STATE ACT 1952- SECT 4  Number of Ministers 
The number of the Ministers of State must not exceed:

 
<dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(a)</dt><dd>in the case of those designated, when appointed by the Governor-General,

as Parliamentary Secretary&#151;12; and</dd>

<dt>(b)</dt><dd>in the case of those not so designated&#151;30.

</dd>

</dl></dl>
###  MINISTERS OF STATE ACT 1952- SECT 5  Salaries of Ministers 
The annual sum payable under section 66 of the Constitution for the salaries

of the Ministers of State must not exceed $2,800,000 in a financial year.

 
###  MINISTERS OF STATE ACT 1952- SECT 6  Additional allowances to Ministers of State 
There are payable to the Ministers of State, in addition to their salaries,

and in addition to any allowances payable to them as senators or members of the

House of Representatives, such respective allowances as are determined by the

Remuneration Tribunal in pursuance of the _Remuneration Tribunals Act 1973_.
