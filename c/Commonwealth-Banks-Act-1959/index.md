###  COMMONWEALTH BANKS ACT 1959<part>Part&#160;I&#151;Preliminary </part>

###  COMMONWEALTH BANKS ACT 1959- SECT 1  Short title [_see_ Note 1] 
<dl compact="">

		This Act may be cited as the _Commonwealth Banks Act 1959_.

 </dl>
###  COMMONWEALTH BANKS ACT 1959- SECT 2  Commencement [_see_ Note 1] 
<dl compact="">

		This Act shall come into operation on the day on which the _Reserve Bank Act 1959_ comes into operation.

 </dl>
###  COMMONWEALTH BANKS ACT 1959- SECT 4  Interpretation 
<dl compact="">

		In this Act, unless the contrary intention appears:

 </dl>
<dl compact=""><dl compact="">

_articles_ means articles of association. _Australia_ includes the external Territories. _bank_ means a person carrying on the business of banking, and includes the Commonwealth Bank and the Development Bank. _Board_ means the Board of Directors of the Commonwealth Bank. _Commonwealth Bank_ means the Commonwealth Bank of Australia. _conversion time_ means the commencement of section&#160;22 of the _Commonwealth Banks Restructuring Act 1990_. _Corporation_ means the Commonwealth Bank Officers Superannuation Corporation, including that body as continuing in existence with a different name. _Development Bank_ means the Commonwealth Development Bank of Australia, including that body as continuing in existence with a different name. _memorandum_ means memorandum of association. _pre-conversion time_ means the commencement of section&#160;21 of the _Commonwealth Banks Restructuring Act 1990_. _Securities Commission_ means:  </dl></dl>

<dl compact=""><dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(a)</dt><dd>before the date prescribed for the purposes of paragraph&#160;(b)&#151;the

National Companies and Securities Commission; or</dd>

<dt>(b)</dt><dd>on and after the date prescribed for the purposes of this paragraph&#151;the

Australian Securities Commission.

</dd>

</dl></dl></dl>

<def><dl compact=""><dl compact="">

_share_, in relation to a body corporate, means a share in the

share capital of the body corporate.

 </dl></dl>

**

###  COMMONWEALTH BANKS ACT 1959- SECT 5  Application to Territories 
**

 <dl compact=""><dl compact="">

		This Act extends to all the Territories.

###  COMMONWEALTH BANKS ACT 1959- SECT 27A  Share capital of Commonwealth Bank 
**

 <dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(1)</dt><dd>As from the pre-conversion time, the Commonwealth Bank is to have a share

capital divided into shares of $2 each or such other amount as is prescribed.</dd> <dt>(2)</dt><dd>The amount of the share capital is equal to the amount required to be applied under section&#160;27B in paying up shares in the Commonwealth Bank.</dd> <dt>(3)</dt><dd>The share capital may be divided into classes of shares.</dd> <dt>(4)</dt><dd>Rights may be attached to shares included in a class of shares.</dd> <dt>(5)</dt><dd>As from the conversion time, this section has effect subject to the _Companies Act 1981_. </dd> </dl></dl>

**

###  COMMONWEALTH BANKS ACT 1959- SECT 27B  Shares to be paid up and issued 
**

 <dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(1)</dt><dd>As soon as practicable after the pre-conversion time, the Commonwealth

Bank must apply its capital, calculated immediately before the pre-conversion

time, in paying up in full shares in the Commonwealth Bank.</dd> <dt>(2)</dt><dd>If the amount of the capital is not a multiple of the nominal value of a share, subsection&#160;(1) applies as if the amount were reduced to the nearest multiple of the nominal value of a share.</dd> <dt>(3)</dt><dd>As soon as practicable after complying with subsection&#160;(1), the Commonwealth Bank must issue to the Commonwealth all the shares paid up under that subsection.</dd> <dt>(4)</dt><dd>The shares issued under subsection&#160;(3) are to be taken to have been issued for valuable consideration other than cash.</dd> <dt>(5)</dt><dd>The Commonwealth is not a member of Commonwealth Bank at any time before the conversion time merely because the Commonwealth holds shares in Commonwealth Bank. </dd> </dl></dl>

###  COMMONWEALTH BANKS ACT 1959- SECT 27H  Commonwealth Bank is not a Commonwealth authority or Commonwealth company etc. 
**

<dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(1)</dt><dd>This section has effect for the purposes of the laws of the Commonwealth,

a State or a Territory. However, the effect of this section is subject to any

express provision in the law concerned that refers specifically to the

Commonwealth Bank.</dd> <dt>(2)</dt><dd>The Commonwealth Bank is to be taken: </dd> </dl></dl>

<dl compact=""><dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(a)</dt><dd>not to have been incorporated or established for a public purpose or for a

purpose of the Commonwealth; and</dd>

<dt>(b)</dt><dd>not to be a public authority or an instrumentality or agency of the Crown;

and</dd>

<dt>(c)</dt><dd>without limiting paragraphs&#160;(a) and (b), not to be a public authority

under the Commonwealth for the purposes of the _Crimes Act 1914_.

</dd>

</dl></dl></dl>

<dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(3)</dt><dd>The Commonwealth is to be taken:

</dd> </dl></dl>

<dl compact=""><dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(a)</dt><dd>not to have a controlling interest or substantial interest in the

Commonwealth Bank; and</dd>

<dt>(b)</dt><dd>not to be in a position to control the Commonwealth Bank.

</dd>

</dl></dl></dl>

###  COMMONWEALTH BANKS ACT 1959- SECT 27J  Operation of section&#160;25B of Acts Interpretation Act

###  COMMONWEALTH BANKS ACT 1959- SECT 28  General functions of Commonwealth Bank 
**

 <dl compact=""><dl compact="">
