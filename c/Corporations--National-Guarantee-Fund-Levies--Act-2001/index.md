###  CORPORATIONS (NATIONAL GUARANTEE FUND LEVIES) ACT 2001- SECT 1  Short title [_see_ Note 1] 
This Act may be cited as the _Corporations (National Guarantee Fund Levies) Act 2001_.

 
###  CORPORATIONS (NATIONAL GUARANTEE FUND LEVIES) ACT 2001- SECT 2  Commencement [_see_ Note 1] 
This Act commences at the same time as the _Corporations Act 2001_.

 
###  CORPORATIONS (NATIONAL GUARANTEE FUND LEVIES) ACT 2001- SECT 3  Definitions 
Expressions used in this Act that are defined in a provision of the

_Corporations Act 2001_ that applies for the purposes of Division&#160;4

of Part&#160;7.5 of that Act (whether it also applies for other purposes) have

the same meanings in this Act as they are given by that provision.

###  CORPORATIONS (NATIONAL GUARANTEE FUND LEVIES) ACT 2001- SECT 5  Amount of levy 
<dt>(1)</dt><dd>A determination referred to in subsection 889J(1) of the _Corporations Act 2001_ must specify, or specify a method for determining, the amount of

levy that is to be payable. The amount of levy imposed by paragraph 4(a) of

this Act in relation to the determination is the amount specified in, or worked

out in accordance with the method specified in, the determination.</dd> <dt>(2)</dt><dd>A determination referred to in subsection 889K(1) of the _Corporations Act 2001_ must specify, or specify a method for determining, the amount of levy that is to be payable. The amount of levy imposed by paragraph 4(b) of this Act in relation to the determination is the amount specified in, or worked out in accordance with the method specified in, the determination.</dd> <dt>(3)</dt><dd>Different amounts or methods may be specified in the same determination (whether it is a determination referred to in subsection 889J(1) or a determination referred to in subsection 889K(1)) in relation to different classes of matters of things. </dd>
