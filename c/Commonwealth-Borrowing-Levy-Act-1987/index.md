###  Long Title 
**
###  COMMONWEALTH BORROWING LEVY ACT 1987- SECT 1  Short title [_see_ Note 1] 
This Act may be cited as the _Commonwealth Borrowing Levy Act 1987_.

 
###  COMMONWEALTH BORROWING LEVY ACT 1987- SECT 2  Commencement [_see_ Note 1] 
This Act shall come into operation on the day on which it receives the Royal

Assent.

 
###  COMMONWEALTH BORROWING LEVY ACT 1987- SECT 3  Interpretation 
<dt>(1)</dt><dd>In this Act:

</dd> 
<dl compact=""><dl compact="">

_body_ means:

 </dl></dl>

<dl compact=""><dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(a)</dt><dd>a body referred to in the Schedule; and</dd>

<dt>(b)</dt><dd>a body declared by another Act to be a prescribed body for the purposes of

this Act; and</dd>

<dt>(c)</dt><dd>a body corporate (other than a body referred to in the Schedule) that is,

under the _Corporations Act 2001_, a subsidiary of a body referred to in

paragraph&#160;(a) or (b).

</dd>

</dl></dl></dl>

<dl compact="">

<dt>(2)</dt><dd>Expressions have the same meaning in this Act as in the _Commonwealth Borrowing Levy Collection Act 1987_.

</dd> </dl>

**

###  COMMONWEALTH BORROWING LEVY ACT 1987- SECT 4  Application of Act 
**

 <dl compact="">

		This Act applies to borrowings of money, otherwise than from the

Commonwealth, undertaken on or after 1&#160;July 1987 by a body, including,

without limiting the generality of the foregoing, such a borrowing made wholly

or partly in a currency other than Australian currency.

 </dl>

**

###  COMMONWEALTH BORROWING LEVY ACT 1987- SECT 5  Imposition of levy 
**

 <dl compact="">

		A levy is imposed on all borrowings to which this Act applies, other than

such of those borrowings, or such parts of those borrowings, as are exempted

from the levy by the regulations.

 </dl>

**

###  COMMONWEALTH BORROWING LEVY ACT 1987- SECT 6  Rate of levy 
**

 <dl compact="">

		The rate of the levy is such annual rate as is prescribed, being an annual

rate not exceeding 0.5% of the amount in respect of which the levy is payable.

 </dl>

<center>Back to Top</center>

**

###  COMMONWEALTH BORROWING LEVY ACT 1987- SECT 7  By whom levy payable 
**

 <dl compact="">

		The levy is payable by the body which undertakes the borrowing.

 </dl>

**

###  COMMONWEALTH BORROWING LEVY ACT 1987- SECT 8  Regulations  
**

 <dl compact="">

		The Governor-General may make regulations, not inconsistent with this Act,

prescribing matters required or permitted by this Act to be prescribed.

 </dl>
