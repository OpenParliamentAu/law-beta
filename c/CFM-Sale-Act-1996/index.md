###  CFM SALE ACT 1996<part>Part 1&#151;Preliminary </part>

###  CFM SALE ACT 1996- SECT 1  Short title [_see_ Note 1] 
This Act may be cited as the _CFM Sale Act 1996_.

 
###  CFM SALE ACT 1996- SECT 2  Commencement [_see_ Note 1] 
<dt>(1)</dt><dd>Subject to this section, this Act commences on the day on which it

receives the Royal Assent.</dd> <dt>(2)</dt><dd>Items 3 and 4 of Schedule 1 are taken to have commenced immediately after the commencement of Part 2 of the _Commonwealth Funds Management Limited Act 1990_.</dd> <dt>(3)</dt><dd>Subject to subsection (4), items 1, 2, 5, 6, 7, 8, 9 and 11 of Schedule 1 commence on a day or days to be fixed by Proclamation.</dd> <dt>(4)</dt><dd>If an item of Schedule 1 does not commence under subsection (3) within the period of 6 months beginning on the day on which this Act receives the Royal Assent, it commences on the first day after the end of that period.</dd> <dt>(5)</dt><dd>If this Act commences before the commencement of the _Legislative Instruments Act 1996_, item 1 of Schedule 2 commences immediately after the commencement of the _Legislative Instruments Act 1996_. </dd> 
###  CFM SALE ACT 1996- SECT 3  Definitions 
<dt>(1)</dt><dd>In this Act, unless the contrary intention appears:

</dd> 
<dl compact=""><dl compact="">

_CFM_ means the body corporate known before the day declared under

section 25 as Commonwealth Funds Management Limited:

 </dl></dl>

<dl compact=""><dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(a)</dt><dd>by whatever name called from time to time; and</dd>

<dt>(b)</dt><dd>in whatever form that body corporate takes from time to time.

</dd>

</dl></dl></dl>

<def><dl compact=""><dl compact="">

_CFM body_ means CFM or a transferred body. _CFM subsidiary_ means a body corporate that is a subsidiary of CFM. _related_ _body_ means:  </dl></dl>

<dl compact=""><dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(a)</dt><dd>in relation to CFM&#151;a body corporate that is related to CFM; or</dd>

<dt>(b)</dt><dd>in relation to a transferred body&#151;a body corporate that is related to the

transferred body.

</dd>

</dl></dl></dl>

<def><dl compact=""><dl compact="">

_sale day_ means:

 </dl></dl>

<dl compact=""><dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(a)</dt><dd>in relation to CFM&#151;the day declared under section 25 to be the sale day

for CFM; or</dd>

<dt>(b)</dt><dd>in relation to a transferred body&#151;the day declared under section 26 to be

the sale day for that body.

</dd>

</dl></dl></dl>

<center>Back to Top</center>

<def><dl compact=""><dl compact="">

_share_, in relation to a body corporate, means a

share in the body's share capital. _subsidiary_ has the meaning given by subsection (2). _transferred body_ means a body corporate that was a CFM subsidiary all of the shares in which were transferred by CFM to the Commonwealth in accordance with the Minister's direction under section 7\. _transferred employee_ means a person whose employment is transferred in accordance with a declaration under section 9\. _transfer time_, in relation to an employee, has the meaning given by section 9\. _voting share_ has the same meaning as in the _Corporations Act 2001_.  </dl></dl>

<dl compact="">

<dt>(2)</dt><dd>For the purposes of this Act, the question whether a body corporate is a

subsidiary of another body corporate is to be determined in the same manner as

that question is determined under the _Corporations Act 2001_.</dd> <dt>(3)</dt><dd>For the purposes of this Act, the question whether a body corporate is related to another body corporate is to be determined in the same manner as that question is determined under the _Corporations Act 2001_. </dd> </dl>

###  CFM SALE ACT 1996<part>Part 2&#151;Transfer of CFM subsidiaries to the Commonwealth </part>
**</center>

**

###  CFM SALE ACT 1996- SECT 5  What this Part is about 
**

 This Part is about the transfer of CFM subsidiaries from CFM to the Commonwealth so that they may be sold under Part 3\. 

<center>**

###  CFM SALE ACT 1996<division>Division 1&#151;Subsidiaries </division> 
**</center>

**

###  CFM SALE ACT 1996- SECT 6  Subsidiaries to be established 
**

 <dl compact="">

		The Minister may, in writing, direct CFM to establish subsidiaries.

 </dl>

**

###  CFM SALE ACT 1996- SECT 7  CFM to transfer shares in subsidiaries to the Commonwealth 
**

<dl compact="">

		The Minister may, in writing, direct CFM to transfer from CFM to the

Commonwealth all of the shares in a CFM subsidiary, including a subsidiary that

existed before the commencement of this Act.

 </dl>

**

###  CFM SALE ACT 1996- SECT 8  Exemption from stamp duty and other taxes 
**

 <dl compact="">

		Stamp duty or other tax is not payable under a law of the Commonwealth, a

State or a Territory in respect of:

 </dl>

<dl compact=""><dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(a)</dt><dd>the transfer of shares in a CFM subsidiary by CFM to the Commonwealth;

or</dd>

<dt>(b)</dt><dd>anything done (including a transaction entered into or an instrument or

document made, executed, lodged or given) because of, or for a purpose

connected with or arising out of, the transfer.

</dd>

</dl></dl></dl>

###  CFM SALE ACT 1996- SECT 9  Transfer of staff from CFM 
**

 <dl compact="">

<dt>(1)</dt><dd>The Minister may, by written instrument, declare that a specified employee

of CFM on a specified day:

</dd> </dl>

<dl compact=""><dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(a)</dt><dd>ceases to be employed by CFM; and</dd>

<dt>(b)</dt><dd>is taken to have been engaged by a specified CFM subsidiary or a specified

transferred body as an employee of that subsidiary or body.

</dd>

</dl></dl></dl>

<dl compact="">

<dt>(2)</dt><dd>The declaration has effect accordingly.</dd> <dt>(3)</dt><dd>The employee's _transfer time_ occurs immediately after the beginning of the day specified in the declaration. </dd> </dl>

**

###  CFM SALE ACT 1996- SECT 10  Terms and conditions of transferred employees 
**

 <dl compact="">

<dt>(1)</dt><dd>A person who is a transferred employee is taken:

</dd> </dl>

<dl compact=""><dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(a)</dt><dd>to have been engaged by the CFM subsidiary or the transferred body on the

same terms and conditions as those that applied to the person, immediately

before the employee's transfer time, as an employee of CFM; and</dd>

<dt>(b)</dt><dd>to have accrued an entitlement to benefits, in connection with that

engagement by the CFM subsidiary or the transferred body, that is equivalent to

the entitlement that the person had accrued, as an employee of CFM, immediately

before the employee's transfer time.

</dd>

</dl></dl></dl>

<dl compact="">

<dt>(2)</dt><dd>The service of a transferred employee as an employee of the CFM subsidiary

or the transferred body is taken, for all purposes, to have been continuous

with the service of the employee, immediately before the employee's transfer

time, as an employee of CFM.</dd> <dt>(3)</dt><dd>A transferred employee is not entitled to receive any payment or any other benefit merely because he or she stopped being an employee of CFM as a result of this Division. </dd> </dl>

**

###  CFM SALE ACT 1996- SECT 11  Variation of terms and conditions of employment 
**

 <dl compact="">

<dt>(1)</dt><dd>This Division does not prevent the terms and conditions of a transferred

employee's employment after the employee's transfer time from being varied:

</dd> </dl>

<dl compact=""><dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(a)</dt><dd>in accordance with those terms and conditions; or</dd>

<dt>(b)</dt><dd>by or under a law, award, determination or agreement.

</dd>

</dl></dl></dl>

<dl compact="">

<dt>(2)</dt><dd>In this section:

</dd> </dl>

<def><dl compact=""><dl compact="">

_vary_, in relation to terms and conditions, includes:

 </dl></dl>

<dl compact=""><dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(a)</dt><dd>omitting any of those terms and conditions; or</dd>

<dt>(b)</dt><dd>adding to those terms and conditions; or</dd>

<dt>(c)</dt><dd>substituting new terms or conditions for any of those terms and conditions.

</dd>

</dl></dl></dl>

**

###  CFM SALE ACT 1996- SECT 12  Statement of accrued benefits 
**

 <dl compact="">

<dt>(1)</dt><dd>Before the employment of an employee of CFM is transferred under section

9, CFM must give the employee a written statement setting out the particulars

of the benefits to which the employee has accrued an entitlement.</dd> <dt>(2)</dt><dd>In any proceedings relating to paragraph 9(1)(b), the statement is prima facie evidence of the matters set out in the statement.</dd> <dt>(3)</dt><dd>A failure by CFM to comply with subsection (1) does not affect the validity of the transfer of the employment of the employee. </dd> </dl>

###  CFM SALE ACT 1996- SECT 27  What this Division is about 
**

 This Division (other than Subdivision 2F) is about the long service leave that may be granted to an employee of a CFM body who had less than 10 years service when the body was sold and who continued, after the sale, as an employee of that body or a related body. Subdivision 2F deals with employees who had at least 10 years service.

###  CFM SALE ACT 1996<division>Subdivision 2B&#151;Long service leave for employees who attain at least 10 years service </division>

**

###  CFM SALE ACT 1996- SECT 31  What this Subdivision is about 
**

 This Subdivision is about the long service leave that may be granted to an employee of a CFM body or a related body who attains at least 10 years service. It also deals with employees who reach minimum retiring age or are retrenched. 

**

###  CFM SALE ACT 1996- SECT 32  Employees who attain 10 years service 
**

 <dl compact="">

<dt>(1)</dt><dd>A CFM body or a related body may grant an employee long service leave on

full salary for a period up to the employee's long service leave credit under

subsection 43(1) if:

</dd> </dl>

<dl compact=""><dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(a)</dt><dd>the employee was an employee of the CFM body immediately before the body's

sale day; and</dd>

<dt>(b)</dt><dd>at that time, the employee's period of service for the purposes of the

Long Service Leave Act was less than 10 years; and</dd>

<dt>(c)</dt><dd>from and after the body's sale day, the employee continued to be employed

by the CFM body or a related body until his or her combined service period was

at least 10 years.

</dd>

</dl></dl></dl>

<dl compact="">

<dt>(2)</dt><dd>The CFM body or related body (as the case requires) may, if the employee

asks in writing, grant the employee long service leave on half salary for a

period not exceeding twice the period that could have been granted to the

employee on full salary.

</dd> </dl>

**

###  CFM SALE ACT 1996- SECT 33  Employees reaching minimum retiring age or being retrenched 
**

<dl compact="">

<dt>(1)</dt><dd>A CFM body or a related body may grant an employee long service leave on

full salary for a period up to the employee's long service leave credit under

subsection 43(1) if:

</dd> </dl>

<dl compact=""><dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(a)</dt><dd>the employee was an employee of the CFM body immediately before the body's

sale day; and</dd>

<dt>(b)</dt><dd>at that time, the employee's period of service for the purposes of the

Long Service Leave Act was less than 10 years; and</dd>

<dt>(c)</dt><dd>from and after the body's sale day, the employee continued to be employed

by the CFM body or a related body; and</dd>

<dt>(d)</dt><dd>the employee stopped being an employee of the CFM body or a related body,

on or after reaching the minimum retiring age, or because of retrenchment; and

</dd>

<dt>(e)</dt><dd>the employee's combined service period at the time when he or she stopped

being an employee of the CFM body or a related body was at least one year.

</dd>

</dl></dl></dl>

<center>Back to Top</center>

<dl compact="">

<dt>(2)</dt><dd>Long service leave granted under this section is to be taken so as to end

immediately before the employee stopped being an employee.</dd> <dt>(3)</dt><dd>The CFM body or related body (as the case requires) may, if the employee asks in writing, grant the employee long service leave on half salary for a period not exceeding twice the period that could have been granted to the employee on full salary. </dd> </dl>

**

 </dl>

</dd>

###  CFM SALE ACT 1996<part>Part 4&#151;Transfer of assets, liabilities, rights and obligations of CFM bodies </part>
**</center>

**

###  CFM SALE ACT 1996- SECT 63  What this Part is about 
**

 This Part is about the transfer of assets, liabilities, rights and obligations of CFM bodies. The transfers are to be effected by way of Ministerial declaration. 

<center>**

###  CFM SALE ACT 1996<division>Division 1&#151;Preliminary </division> 
**</center>

**

###  CFM SALE ACT 1996- SECT 64  Definitions 
**

 <dl compact="">

		In this Part, unless the contrary intention appears:

 </dl>

<center>Back to Top</center>

<def><dl compact=""><dl compact="">

_asset_ means:

 </dl></dl>

<dl compact=""><dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(a)</dt><dd>any legal or equitable estate or interest in real or personal property,

including a contingent or prospective one; and</dd>

<dt>(b)</dt><dd>any right, privilege or immunity, including a contingent or prospective

one.

</dd>

</dl></dl></dl>

<def><dl compact=""><dl compact="">

_contract_ includes:

 </dl></dl>

<dl compact=""><dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(a)</dt><dd>a deed; and</dd>

<dt>(b)</dt><dd>a deed poll.

</dd>

</dl></dl></dl>

<def><dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dl compact=""><dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(a)</dt><dd>in relation to an asset to which a declaration under subsection 65(1)

relates&#151;the Commonwealth, a CFM subsidiary or a transferred body, whichever is

###  CFM SALE ACT 1996<division>Division 2&#151;Transfer of assets </division> 
**</center>

**

###  CFM SALE ACT 1996- SECT 65  Transfer of assets  
**

 <dl compact="">

<dt>(1)</dt><dd>The Minister may, by written instrument, declare that a specified asset of

a CFM body (other than shares in the CFM body) vests in a specified transferee

on a specified day without any conveyance, transfer or assignment.</dd> <dt>(2)</dt><dd>The declaration has effect accordingly.</dd> <dt>(3)</dt><dd>The Minister must as soon as practicable, but no later than 21 days after making the declaration, publish the declaration in the _Gazette_.</dd> <dt>(4)</dt><dd>Any consideration payable for the transfer of an asset under this section is payable to the Commonwealth instead of to the CFM body. </dd> </dl>

<dl compact=""><dl compact="">

Note:	An asset or instrument may be specified by name, by

inclusion in a specified class or in any other way.

 </dl></dl>

**

###  CFM SALE ACT 1996- SECT 66  References to CFM body in instruments relating to transferred assets 
**

<dl compact="">

		An instrument relating to an asset of a CFM body specified in a declaration

continues to have effect after the asset vests in the specified transferee as

if a reference in the instrument to the CFM body were a reference to the

specified transferee.

 </dl>

**

###  CFM SALE ACT 1996- SECT 67  Transferees are successors in law of CFM body 
**

 <dl compact="">

		Immediately after an asset vests in the specified transferee under a

declaration, the specified transferee becomes the CFM body's successor in law

in relation to the asset.

 </dl>

<center>Back to Top</center>

###  CFM SALE ACT 1996<division>Division 3&#151;Transfer of rights and obligations </division> 
**</center>

**

###  CFM SALE ACT 1996- SECT 68  Transfer of rights and obligations 
**

 <dl compact="">

<dt>(1)</dt><dd>The Minister may, by written instrument, declare that the rights and

obligations of a CFM body under a specified contract on a specified day:

</dd> </dl>

<dl compact=""><dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(a)</dt><dd>cease to be rights and obligations of the CFM body; and</dd>

<dt>(b)</dt><dd>become rights and obligations of the specified transferee.

</dd>

</dl></dl></dl>

<dl compact="">

<dt>(2)</dt><dd>The declaration has effect accordingly.</dd> <dt>(3)</dt><dd>A Minister must as soon as practicable, but no later than 21 days after making the declaration, publish the declaration in the _Gazette_. </dd> </dl>

<dl compact=""><dl compact="">

Note:	A contract or instrument may be specified by name, by

inclusion in a specified class or in any other way.

 </dl></dl>

**

###  CFM SALE ACT 1996- SECT 69  References to CFM body in instruments relating to transferred rights and obligations 
**

<dl compact="">

		An instrument relating to a contract specified in a declaration continues to

have effect after the rights and obligations of a CFM body under the contract

become rights and obligations of the specified transferee as if a reference in

the instrument to the CFM body were a reference to the specified transferee.

 </dl>

**

###  CFM SALE ACT 1996- SECT 70  Transferees are successors in law of CFM body 
**

 <dl compact="">

		Immediately after rights and obligations become rights and obligations of the

specified transferee under a declaration, the specified transferee becomes the

CFM body's successor in law in relation to those rights and obligations.

 </dl>

###  CFM SALE ACT 1996- SECT 71  Transfer of liabilities 
**

 <dl compact="">

<dt>(1)</dt><dd>The Minister may, by written instrument, declare that a specified

liability of a CFM body (other than a liability under a contract) on a

specified day:

</dd> </dl>

<dl compact=""><dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(a)</dt><dd>ceases to be a liability of the CFM body; and</dd>

<dt>(b)</dt><dd>becomes a liability of a specified transferee.

</dd>

</dl></dl></dl>

<dl compact="">

<dt>(2)</dt><dd>The declaration has effect accordingly.</dd> <dt>(3)</dt><dd>The Minister must as soon as practicable, but no later than 21 days after making the declaration, publish the declaration in the _Gazette_. </dd> </dl>

<dl compact=""><dl compact="">

Note:	A liability or instrument may be specified by name, by

inclusion in a specified class or in any other way.

 </dl></dl>

**

###  CFM SALE ACT 1996- SECT 72  References to CFM body in instruments relating to transferred liabilities 
**

<dl compact="">

		An instrument relating to a liability of a CFM body specified in a

declaration continues to have effect after the liability becomes a liability of

the specified transferee as if a reference in the instrument to the CFM body

were a reference to the specified transferee.

 </dl>

<center>Back to Top</center>

**

###  CFM SALE ACT 1996- SECT 73  Transferees are successors in law of CFM body 
**

 <dl compact="">

		Immediately after a liability becomes a liability of the specified transferee

under a declaration, the specified transferee becomes the CFM body's successor

in law in relation to the liability.

 </dl>

###  CFM SALE ACT 1996- SECT 74  Exemption from stamp duty and other taxes  
**

 <dl compact="">

<dt>(1)</dt><dd>Stamp duty or other tax is not payable under a law of the Commonwealth, a

State or a Territory in respect of:

</dd> </dl>

<dt>(b)</dt><dd>anything done (including a transaction entered into or an instrument or

</dd>

</dl></dl></dl>

<dl compact="">

<dt>(2)</dt><dd>In this section:

</dd> </dl>

_exempt matter_ means:

 </dl></dl>

<dt>(a)</dt><dd>the transfer of an asset under section 65; or</dd>

<dt>(b)</dt><dd>the transfer of rights and obligations under section 68; or</dd>

<dt>(c)</dt><dd>the transfer of a liability under section 71.

</dd>

###  CFM SALE ACT 1996- SECT 75  Application of the _Income Tax Assessment Act 1936_ in relation to certain transfers

<dl compact="">

</dd> </dl>

<dl compact=""><dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(a)</dt><dd>in the case of a transferred body&#151;after the day on which all of the

<dt>(b)</dt><dd>in the case of CFM&#151;after the day on which all of the shares in Total Risk

Management Pty. Limited are transferred by CFM to the Commonwealth in

accordance with the Minister's direction under section 7, but on or before

CFM's sale day.

</dd>

</dl></dl></dl>

<dl compact=""><dl compact="">

Note:	Total Risk Management Pty. Limited was a subsidiary of CFM

when this Act received the Royal Assent.

 </dl></dl>

<dl compact="">

<dt>(2)</dt><dd>The _Income Tax Assessment Act 1936_ applies to the CFM body and the

specified transferee as if:

</dd> </dl>

<dl compact=""><dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(a)</dt><dd>the specified transferee had acquired the asset, liability, right or

obligation at the time when, and in the circumstances in which, the CFM body

acquired the asset, liability, right or obligation; and</dd>

<dt>(b)</dt><dd>the specified transferee had treated and dealt with the asset, liability,

right or obligation in the way that the CFM body treated and dealt with the

asset, liability, right or obligation; and</dd>

<dt>(c)</dt><dd>the CFM body had never acquired or disposed of the asset, liability, right

or obligation.

</dd>

</dl></dl></dl>

<center>Back to Top</center>

<dl compact="">

<dt>(3)</dt><dd>Subsection (2) does not affect the application of the _Income Tax Assessment Act 1936_ in relation to anything that happened in relation to

the asset, liability, right or obligation before it was transferred from the

CFM body to the specified transferee.</dd> <dt>(4)</dt><dd>Subsection (2) does not affect the nature or effect of the transfer for a purpose other than a purpose of the _Income Tax Assessment Act 1936_. </dd> </dl>

###  CFM SALE ACT 1996- SECT 79  Compensation&#151;constitutional safety net 
**

 <dl compact="">

<dt>(1)</dt><dd>If:

</dd> </dl>

<dl compact=""><dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(a)</dt><dd>apart from this section, the operation of this Act would result in the

<dt>(b)</dt><dd>the acquisition would be invalid because of paragraph 51(xxxi) of the

</dd>

</dl></dl></dl>

the Commonwealth is liable to pay compensation of a reasonable amount to the person in respect of the acquisition.

<dl compact="">

<dt>(2)</dt><dd>If the Commonwealth and the person do not agree on the amount of the

Australia for the recovery from the Commonwealth of such reasonable amount of

compensation as the Court determines.</dd> <dt>(3)</dt><dd>Any damages or compensation recovered, or other remedy given, in a proceeding begun otherwise than under this section must be taken into account in assessing compensation payable in a proceeding begun under this section and arising out of the same event or transaction.</dd> <dt>(4)</dt><dd>In this section: </dd> </dl>

<def><dl compact=""><dl compact="">

_acquisition of property_ has the same meaning as in paragraph

51(xxxi) of the Constitution. _just terms_ has the same meaning as in paragraph 51(xxxi) of the Constitution.  </dl></dl>

</dd>

<dt>(ii)</dt><dd>the disclosure of any information; or

</dd>

</dl></dl></dl></dl>

<dl compact=""><dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(d)</dt><dd>releasing any surety from any of the surety's obligations in relation to a

liability or obligation that is transferred under a declaration made under Part

4.

</dd>

</dl></dl></dl>
