###  Long Title 
**
Commonwealth Borrowing Levy Act 1987

###  COMMONWEALTH BORROWING LEVY COLLECTION ACT 1987- SECT 1  Short title [_see_ Note 1] 
This Act may be cited as the _Commonwealth Borrowing Levy Collection Act 1987_.

 
###  COMMONWEALTH BORROWING LEVY COLLECTION ACT 1987- SECT 2  Commencement [_see_ Note 1] 
<dt>(1)</dt><dd>Section&#160;6 shall be deemed to have come into operation on 1&#160;July

1987.</dd> <dt>(2)</dt><dd>The remaining provisions of this Act shall come into operation on the day on which this Act receives the Royal Assent. </dd> 
###  COMMONWEALTH BORROWING LEVY COLLECTION ACT 1987- SECT 3  Interpretation 
<dt>(1)</dt><dd>In this Act:

</dd> 
<dl compact=""><dl compact="">

_body_ has the same meaning as in the Levy Act. _borrowing_ includes the raising of money by dealing in securities. _Levy Act_ means the _Commonwealth Borrowing Levy Act 1987_. _securities_ includes stocks, debentures, debenture stocks, notes, bonds, promissory notes, bills of exchange and similar instruments or documents.  </dl></dl>

<dl compact="">

<dt>(2)</dt><dd>A reference in this Act to dealing with securities includes a reference to:

</dd> </dl>

<dl compact=""><dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(a)</dt><dd>creating, executing, entering into, drawing, making, accepting, endorsing,

issuing, discounting, selling, purchasing or re-selling securities;</dd>

<dt>(b)</dt><dd>creating, selling, purchasing or re-selling rights or options in respect

of securities; and</dd>

<dt>(c)</dt><dd>entering into agreements or other arrangements relating to securities.

</dd>

</dl></dl></dl>

<dl compact="">

<dt>(3)</dt><dd>Subject to subsection&#160;(4):

</dd> </dl>

<dl compact=""><dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(a)</dt><dd>the issue by a body of an instrument acknowledging a debt in consideration

of the payment or deposit of money or of the provision of credit; or</dd>

<dt>(b)</dt><dd>the obtaining of credit by the body;

</dd>

</dl></dl></dl>

shall, for the purposes of this Act, be deemed to be a borrowing by the body to the extent of the amount of that money or of that credit, as the case may be.

<dl compact="">

<dt>(4)</dt><dd>The issue of an instrument of the kind referred to in

paragraph&#160;(3)(a), or the obtaining of credit, by a body other than a body

referred to in item&#160;5 or 14 of the Schedule to the Levy Act in relation to

a transaction that is in the ordinary course of the day-to-day operations of

the body shall be deemed not to be a borrowing by the body.

</dd> </dl>

###  COMMONWEALTH BORROWING LEVY COLLECTION ACT 1987- SECT 4  Collection of levy 
**

 <dl compact="">

<dt>(1)</dt><dd>The Treasurer may, by determination published in the _Gazette_:

</dd> </dl>

<dl compact=""><dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(a)</dt><dd>specify the times when and the manner in which the levy is to be paid;

and</dd>

<dt>(b)</dt><dd>require a specified officer of a body to provide the Treasurer, at

specified times, with a statement certifying:

</dd>

</dl></dl></dl>

<dl compact=""><dl compact=""><dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(i)</dt><dd>the amount of the levy payable by the body at a specified time;</dd>

<dt>(ii)</dt><dd>the amount of borrowings on which the amount of the levy was calculated;

and</dd>

<dt>(iii)</dt><dd>such other particulars of borrowings by the body relevant to the

calculation of the levy as are specified in the determination.

</dd>

</dl></dl></dl></dl>

<dl compact="">

<dt>(2)</dt><dd>The provisions of sections&#160;48 (other than paragraph&#160;(1)(a) and

subsection&#160;(2)), 48A, 48B, 49, 49A and 50 of the _Acts Interpretation Act 1901_ apply in relation to determinations as if references in those

provisions to regulations were references to determinations.</dd> <dt>(3)</dt><dd>Determinations shall be deemed not to be Statutory Rules within the meaning of the _Statutory Rules Publication Act 1903_. </dd> </dl>

**

###  COMMONWEALTH BORROWING LEVY COLLECTION ACT 1987- SECT 5  Regulations 
**

 <dl compact="">

		The Governor-General may make regulations, not inconsistent with this Act,

prescribing all matters necessary or convenient to be prescribed for carrying

out or giving effect to this Act.

 </dl>

**

###  COMMONWEALTH BORROWING LEVY COLLECTION ACT 1987- SECT 6  Repeal of Commonwealth Guarantees (Charges) Act 
**

 <dl compact="">

<dt>(1)</dt><dd>The _Commonwealth Guarantees (Charges) Act 1987_ is repealed.</dd> <dt>(2)</dt><dd>In spite of the repeal of the _Commonwealth Guarantees (Charges) Act 1987_, that Act continues to apply to borrowings and raisings of money, within the meaning of that Act, undertaken before 1&#160;July 1987 by a body referred to in the Schedule to that Act.</dd> <dt>(3)</dt><dd>In its application in accordance with subsection&#160;(2), the _Commonwealth Guarantees (Charges) Act 1987_ has effect as if Item&#160;3 in Part A of the Schedule to that Act were omitted. </dd> </dl>
