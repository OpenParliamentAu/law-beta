###  DEFENCE HOUSING AUTHORITY ACT 1987- SECT 1  Short title [_see_ Note 1] 
This Act may be cited as the _Defence Housing Authority Act 1987_.

 
###  DEFENCE HOUSING AUTHORITY ACT 1987- SECT 2  Commencement [_see_ Note 1] 
This Act shall come into operation on a day to be fixed by Proclamation.

###  DEFENCE HOUSING AUTHORITY ACT 1987- SECT 4  Establishment 
**

 <dl compact="">

		An authority by the name of the Defence Housing Authority is established by

this section.

 </dl>

**

###  DEFENCE HOUSING AUTHORITY ACT 1987- SECT 5  Function 
**

 <dl compact="">

<dt>(1)</dt><dd>The function of the Authority is to provide adequate and suitable housing

for:

</dd> </dl>

<dl compact=""><dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(a)</dt><dd>members of the Defence Force and their families;</dd>

<dt>(b)</dt><dd>officers and employees of the Department and their families; and</dd>

<dt>(c)</dt><dd>other persons;

</dd>

</dl></dl></dl>

in order to meet the operational needs of the Defence Force and the requirements of the Department.

<dl compact="">

<dt>(2)</dt><dd>The Authority shall provide such housing as the Minister, by notice in

writing given to the Authority, directs is necessary for the Authority to

provide in order to meet the operational needs of the Defence Force and the

requirements of the Department.

</dd> </dl>

**

###  DEFENCE HOUSING AUTHORITY ACT 1987- SECT 6  Manner of performing function 
**

 <dl compact="">

		The Authority shall endeavour to perform its function in a manner that:

 </dl>

<dl compact=""><dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(a)</dt><dd>is in accordance with the policies of the Commonwealth; and</dd>

<dt>(b)</dt><dd>is in accordance with sound commercial practice.

</dd>

</dl></dl></dl>

**

###  DEFENCE HOUSING AUTHORITY ACT 1987- SECT 7  Powers 
**

 <dl compact="">

<dt>(1)</dt><dd>The Authority has power to do all things necessary or convenient to be

done for, or in connection with, the performance of its functions and, in

particular, may:

</dd> </dl>

<dl compact=""><dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(a)</dt><dd>purchase and otherwise acquire, and sell and otherwise dispose of:

</dd>

</dl></dl></dl>

<dl compact=""><dl compact=""><dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(i)</dt><dd>land and any interest in land; and</dd>

<dt>(ii)</dt><dd>houses;

</dd>

</dl></dl></dl></dl>

<dl compact=""><dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(b)</dt><dd>develop land;</dd>

<dt>(c)</dt><dd>build, demolish, alter, renovate, maintain, repair and improve houses;</dd>

<dt>(d)</dt><dd>convert buildings and other structures that are not houses into houses;</dd>

<dt>(e)</dt><dd>rent out, and generally manage and control:

</dd>

</dl></dl></dl>

<center>Back to Top</center>

<dl compact=""><dl compact=""><dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(i)</dt><dd>land and houses acquired by it; and</dd>

<dt>(ii)</dt><dd>land and houses that are:

</dd>

</dl></dl></dl></dl>

	(A)	owned or held under lease by the Commonwealth or a State or Territory; and
 	(B)	made available to it;

<dl compact=""><dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(f)</dt><dd>determine and collect rents, fees and charges in relation to land and

houses rented out by it;</dd>

<dt>(g)</dt><dd>evict tenants and otherwise recover possession of land and houses referred

to in paragraph (e);</dd>

<dt>(h)</dt><dd>provide and improve amenities, facilities and services for persons living

in houses rented out by it;</dd>

<dt>(j)</dt><dd>purchase and take on hire, and dispose of, plant, machinery, equipment and

other goods;</dd>

<dt>(k)</dt><dd>hire out plant, machinery, equipment and other goods not immediately

required by it;</dd>

<dt>(m)</dt><dd>make charges for work done, and services rendered, by it;</dd>

<dt>(n)</dt><dd>provide transport, accommodation, amenities, facilities and services for

members of the staff of the Authority and their families;</dd>

<dt>(p)</dt><dd>enter into contracts;</dd>

<dt>(q)</dt><dd>appoint agents and attorneys and act as an agent for other persons;</dd>

<dt>(r)</dt><dd>engage consultants;</dd>

<dt>(s)</dt><dd>form, and participate in the formation of, companies;</dd>

<dt>(t)</dt><dd>enter into partnerships;</dd>

<dt>(u)</dt><dd>participate in joint ventures;</dd>

<dt>(w)</dt><dd>accept gifts, grants, bequests and devises made to it and act as trustee

of money and other property vested in it on trust; and</dd>

<dt>(x)</dt><dd>do anything incidental to any of its powers.

</dd>

</dl></dl></dl>

<dl compact="">

<dt>(2)</dt><dd>The Authority shall not, except with the written approval of the Minister,

enter into a contract involving the payment by it of an amount exceeding

$6,000,000.

</dd> </dl>

###  DEFENCE HOUSING AUTHORITY ACT 1987- SECT 8  Limitations on formation of companies etc. 
**

 <dl compact="">

<dt>(1)</dt><dd>The Authority shall not, without the written approval of the Minister:

</dd> </dl>

<dl compact=""><dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(a)</dt><dd>subscribe for or purchase shares in, or debentures or other securities of,

a company; or</dd>

<dt>(b)</dt><dd>form, or participate in the formation of, a company that would, upon its

formation, be a subsidiary of the Authority.

</dd>

</dl></dl></dl>

<dl compact="">

<dt>(2)</dt><dd>An approval under subsection (1):

</dd> </dl>

<dl compact=""><dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(a)</dt><dd>may be of general application or may relate to a particular company or

proposed company; and</dd>

<dt>(b)</dt><dd>may be given subject to specified conditions and restrictions.

</dd>

</dl></dl></dl>

<dl compact="">

<dt>(3)</dt><dd>Subject to subsection (4), where the Authority subscribes for or purchases

shares in, or debentures or other securities of, a company, the Minister shall:

</dd> </dl>

<dl compact=""><dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(a)</dt><dd>cause to be prepared a statement setting out particulars of, and the

reasons for, the subscription or purchase; and</dd>

<dt>(b)</dt><dd>cause a copy of the statement to be laid before each House of Parliament

within 15 sitting days of that House after:

</dd>

</dl></dl></dl>

<dl compact=""><dl compact=""><dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(i)</dt><dd>subject to subparagraph (ii), the subscription or purchase takes place;

or</dd>

<dt>(ii)</dt><dd>if the Minister is of the opinion that the disclosure of the subscription

or purchase would adversely affect the commercial interests of the Authority,

the Minister ceases to be of that opinion.

</dd>

</dl></dl></dl></dl>

<center>Back to Top</center>

<dl compact="">

<dt>(4)</dt><dd>Where the Authority holds a controlling interest in a company, the

Authority shall endeavour to ensure that the company does not do anything that

the Authority itself is not empowered to do.</dd> <dt>(5)</dt><dd>Without limiting the generality of subsection (4), the Authority shall endeavour to ensure that a company in which it holds a controlling interest does not: </dd> </dl>

<dl compact=""><dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(a)</dt><dd>borrow money otherwise than from the Commonwealth; or</dd>

<dt>(b)</dt><dd>raise money otherwise than by borrowing;

</dd>

</dl></dl></dl>

except:

<dl compact=""><dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(c)</dt><dd>with the written approval of the Treasurer; and</dd>

<dt>(d)</dt><dd>on terms and conditions that are specified in, or consistent with, the

approval.

</dd>

</dl></dl></dl>

<dl compact="">

<dt>(6)</dt><dd>Subsection (5) applies to a borrowing or raising of money whether the

money is borrowed or raised by dealing in securities or otherwise, and whether

or not the money is borrowed or raised, in whole or in part, in a currency

other than Australian currency.</dd> <dt>(7)</dt><dd>An approval may be given under subsection (5) in relation to a particular transaction or transactions in a class of transactions.</dd> <dt>(8)</dt><dd>For the purposes of subsection (5): </dd> </dl>

<dl compact=""><dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(a)</dt><dd>the issue by a company of an instrument acknowledging a debt in

consideration of the payment or deposit of money or of the provision of credit;

or</dd>

<dt>(b)</dt><dd>the obtaining of credit by a company;

</dd>

</dl></dl></dl>

shall, to the extent of the amount of that money or of that credit, as the case may be, be deemed to be a borrowing by the company.

<dl compact="">

<dt>(9)</dt><dd>Section 29 of the _Commonwealth Authorities and Companies Act 1997_ does not apply to the Authority.

</dd> </dl>

###  DEFENCE HOUSING AUTHORITY ACT 1987- SECT 11  Authority is body corporate etc. 
**

 <dl compact="">

<dt>(1)</dt><dd>The Authority:

</dd> </dl>

<dl compact=""><dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(a)</dt><dd>is a body corporate with perpetual succession;</dd>

<dt>(b)</dt><dd>shall have a common seal;</dd>

<dt>(c)</dt><dd>may acquire, hold and dispose of real and personal property; and</dd>

<dt>(d)</dt><dd>may sue and be sued in its corporate name.

</dd>

</dl></dl></dl>

<dl compact=""><dl compact="">

Note:	Subject to subsection 8(9), the _Commonwealth Authorities and Companies Act 1997_ applies to the Authority. That Act deals with

matters relating to Commonwealth authorities, including reporting and

accountability, banking and investment, and conduct of officers.

 </dl></dl>

<dl compact="">

<dt>(2)</dt><dd>The common seal of the Authority shall be kept in such custody as the

Authority directs and shall not be used except as authorised by the

Authority.</dd> <dt>(3)</dt><dd>All courts, judges and persons acting judicially shall take judicial notice of the imprint of the common seal of the Authority appearing on a document and shall presume that it was duly affixed. </dd> </dl>

**

 <dl compact="">

 <dl compact="">

 </dl>

**

 <dl compact="">

###  DEFENCE HOUSING AUTHORITY ACT 1987- SECT 52  Resignation 
**

 <dl compact="">

Managing Director and delivered to the Chairperson.

 </dl>

 <dl compact="">

acquires in any business or in any body corporate carrying on any business.

</dd>

</dl></dl></dl>

as if it were a grant, conveyance, memorandum or instrument of transfer of the land (including all rights, title and interest in the land) to the Authority that had been duly executed under the laws in force in the State or Territory.
