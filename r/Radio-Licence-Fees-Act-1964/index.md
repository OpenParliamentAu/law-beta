### <name>BROADCASTING STATIONS LICENCE FEES ACT 1964 - LONG TITLE. </name>
</b>** 

## SECT
<sect> An Act to provide for the payment of Fees in respect of Licences for Commercial<lf> Broadcasting Stations<lf> </lf></lf></sect>
**<b>

### <name>BROADCASTING STATIONS LICENCE FEES ACT 1964 - SECT. 1\. Short title. </name>
</b>** 

## SECT
<sect>   1\. This Act may be cited as the Broadcasting Stations Licence Fees Act 1964.*1* 

<lf> See notes to first article of this Chapter. </lf>
</sect>
**<b>

### <name>BROADCASTING STATIONS LICENCE FEES ACT 1964 - SECT. 2\. Commencement. </name>
</b>** 

## SECT
<sect>   2\. This Act shall come into operation on the day on which it receives the Royal Assent.*1* 

<lf> See notes to first article of this Chapter. </lf>
</sect>
**<b>

### <name>BROADCASTING STATIONS LICENCE FEES ACT 1964 - SECT. 3\. Incorporation of Broadcasting and Television Act. </name>
</b>** 

## SECT
<sect>   3\. The Broadcasting and Television Act 1942-1964 is incorporated and shall be read as one with this Act. 

<lf> </lf>
</sect>
**<b>

### <name>BROADCASTING STATIONS LICENCE FEES ACT 1964 - SECT. 4\. Interpretation. </name>
</b>** 

## SECT
<sect>   4\. (1) In this Act-<lf> <lf>    " gross earnings  ", in relation to a commercial broadcasting station in respect of a period, means the gross earnings of the licensee of the station during that period in respect of the broadcasting from the station of advertisements or other matter, including the gross earnings of the licensee during that period in respect of the provision by him of, or otherwise in respect of, matter broadcast from the station;<lf> <lf>    " licence  " means a licence in respect of a commercial broadcasting station. 

<lf>   (2) Where, in connexion with any transaction, any consideration is paid or given otherwise than in cash, the money value of that consideration shall, for the purposes of this Act, be deemed to have been paid or given. <p><lf> </lf></p></lf>
</lf></lf></lf></lf></sect>
**<b>

### <name>BROADCASTING STATIONS LICENCE FEES ACT 1964 - SECT. 5\. Licence fees. </name>
</b>** 

## SECT
<sect>   5\. There is payable to the Commonwealth by the licensee of a commercial broadcasting station, in respect of his licence, fees in accordance with the next succeeding section. 

<lf> </lf>
</sect>
**<b>

## SECT
<sect>   6.*2* (1) There is payable on the grant of a licence, not being a grant by way of the renewal of a licence, a fee of $200\.

<sect>   *2*, *3*  (2) There is payable on each anniversary of the date of commencement of a licence that occurs during the period of the licence (including any period of renewal or further renewal of the licence) a fee of $200 together with-<lf> <lf>   (a)  an amount equal to 1 per centum of such part of the gross earnings of the station to which the licence relates during the year ended on 30 June last preceding that anniversary as does not exceed $500,000;<lf> <lf>   (b)  an amount equal to 11/2 per centum of such part of those gross earnings as exceeds $500,000 but does not exceed $1,000,000;<lf> <lf>   (c)  an amount equal to 2 per centum of such part of those gross earnings as exceeds $1,000,000 but does not exceed $1,500,000;<lf> <lf>   (d)  an amount equal to 21/2 per centum of such part of those gross earnings as exceeds $1,500,000 but does not exceed $2,000,000;<lf> <lf>   (e)  an amount equal to 3 per centum of such part of those gross earnings as exceeds $2,000,000 but does not exceed $2,500,000;<lf> <lf>   (f)  an amount equal to 31/2 per centum of such part of those gross earnings as exceeds $2,500,000 but does not exceed $3,000,000;<lf> <lf>   (g)  an amount equal to 4 per centum of such part of those gross earnings as exceeds $3,000,000 but does not exceed $3,500,000;<lf> <lf>   (h)  an amount equal to 41/2 per centum of such part of those gross earnings as exceeds $3,500,000 but does not exceed $4,000,000;<lf> <lf>   (j)  an amount equal to 5 per centum of such part of those gross earnings as exceeds $4,000,000 but does not exceed $4,500,000;<lf> <lf>   (k)  an amount equal to 51/2 per centum of such part of those gross earnings as exceeds $4,500,000 but does not exceed $5,000,000; and<lf> <lf>   (l)  an amount equal to 6 per centum of such part of those gross earn ings as exceeds $5,000,000\.

**<b>

### <name>BROADCASTING STATIONS LICENCE FEES ACT 1964 - SECT. 7\. Earnings of subsidiary companies, &c. </name>
</b>** 

## SECT
<sect>   7\. Where the Minister is of the opinion that-<lf> <lf>   (a)  an amount, or part of an amount, earned during any period by a person other than the licensee of a commercial broadcasting station would, if the licensee and that person were the same person, form part of the gross earnings of the station in respect of that period for the purposes of this Act; and<lf> <lf>   (b)  a relationship exists between the licensee and the other person (whether by reason of any shareholding or of any agreement or arrangement, or for any other reason) of such a kind that the amount or the part of the amount, as the case may be, should, for the purposes of this Act, be treated as part of the gross earnings of the station in respect of that period,<lf> <lf> the Minister may direct that the amount or the part of the amount, as the case may be, shall be so treated.
