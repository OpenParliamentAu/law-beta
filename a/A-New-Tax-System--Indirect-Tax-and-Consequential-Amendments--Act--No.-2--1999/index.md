###  A NEW TAX SYSTEM (INDIRECT TAX AND CONSEQUENTIAL AMENDMENTS) ACT (NO. 2) 1999- SECT 1  Short title 
<dl compact="">

		This Act may be cited as the _A New Tax System (Indirect Tax and Consequential Amendments) Act (No. 2) 1999_.

 </dl>
