###  SUPERANNUATION CONTRIBUTIONS TAX (MEMBERS OF CONSTITUTIONALLY PROTECTED SUPERANNUATION FUNDS) IMPOSITION ACT 1997- SECT 1  Short title [_see_ Note 1] 
<dl compact="">

		This Act may be cited as the _Superannuation Contributions Tax (Members of Constitutionally Protected Superannuation Funds) Imposition Act 1997_.

 </dl>
###  SUPERANNUATION CONTRIBUTIONS TAX (MEMBERS OF CONSTITUTIONALLY PROTECTED SUPERANNUATION FUNDS) IMPOSITION ACT 1997- SECT 2  Commencement [_see_ Note 1] 
<dl compact="">

		This Act commences on the day on which it receives the Royal Assent.

 </dl>
###  SUPERANNUATION CONTRIBUTIONS TAX (MEMBERS OF CONSTITUTIONALLY PROTECTED SUPERANNUATION FUNDS) IMPOSITION ACT 1997- SECT 3  Interpretation 
<dl compact="">

		Expressions used in this Act that are defined by the _Superannuation Contributions Tax (Members of Constitutionally Protected Superannuation Funds) Assessment and Collection Act 1997_ have the same meanings as in that Act.

 </dl>
###  SUPERANNUATION CONTRIBUTIONS TAX (MEMBERS OF CONSTITUTIONALLY PROTECTED SUPERANNUATION FUNDS) IMPOSITION ACT 1997- SECT 4  Imposition of superannuation contributions surcharge 
<dl compact="">

		The superannuation contributions surcharge that is payable on a member's

surchargeable contributions for a financial year under the _Superannuation Contributions Tax (Members of Constitutionally Protected Superannuation Funds) Assessment and Collection Act 1997_ is imposed by this Act.

 </dl>
