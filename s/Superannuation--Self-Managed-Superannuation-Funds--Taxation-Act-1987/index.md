### <name>SUPERANNUATION ENTITIES (TAXATION) ACT 1987 - SECT 3 Interpretation </name>
</b>** 

## SECT
<sect>   3.*2* (1) In this Act:<lf>   "Commissioner" means the Insurance and Superannuation Commissioner appointed under the Insurance and Superannuation Commissioner Act 1987, or a person for the time being acting as Insurance and Superannuation Commissioner under that Act;<lf>   "fund or unit trust affected by a reviewable decision", in relation to a reviewable decision, means the fund or unit trust in relation to which the decision was made;<lf>   "reviewable decision" means:<lf>   (f) a decision of the Commissioner under section 15DF; or<lf>   (g) a decision of the Minister under section 15DQ;<lf>   "trustee", in relation to a fund or trust, has the same meaning as in the Superannuation Industry (Supervision) Act 1993;<lf>   "unit trust" has the same meaning as in Part IX of the Tax Act;<lf>   "year of income", in relation to a fund or unit trust, means a period that is, for the purposes of the Tax Act, the year of income of the fund or unit trust that commenced on 1 July 1986 or a subsequent year of income. </lf></lf></lf></lf></lf></lf></lf></lf></sect>
**<b>

### <name>SUPERANNUATION ENTITIES (TAXATION) ACT 1987 - SECT 3A Crown to be bound </name>
</b>** 

## SECT
<sect>   3A. (1) This Act binds the Crown in right of the Commonwealth, of each of the States, of the Australian Capital Territory, of the Northern Territory and of Norfolk Island. 

<lf>   (2) Nothing in this Act renders the Crown in right of the Commonwealth, of a State, of the Australian Capital Territory, of the Northern Territory or of Norfolk Island liable to be prosecuted for an offence. </lf>
</sect>

### <name>SUPERANNUATION ENTITIES (TAXATION) ACT 1987 - SECT 15DAA Definitions </name>
</b>** 

## SECT
<sect>   15DAA. In this Part:<lf>   "basic levy amount" has the meaning given by section 6 of the Superannuation Supervisory Levy Act 1991;<lf>   "late payment penalty" means penalty payable under section 15DC;<lf>   "levy" means the levy imposed by the Superannuation Supervisory Levy Act 1991;<lf>   "minimum basic levy amount", in relation to a year of income, means the lowest basic levy amount applicable to returns for the year of income for funds and unit trusts that are in existence at all times during the year of income. </lf></lf></lf></lf></sect>
**<b>

### <name>SUPERANNUATION ENTITIES (TAXATION) ACT 1987 - SECT 15DA Liability to levy </name>
</b>** 

## SECT
<sect>   15DA. A trustee who lodges a return under section 36 of the Superannuation Industry (Supervision) Act 1993 is liable to pay a levy on that lodgment. </sect>
**<b>

### <name>SUPERANNUATION ENTITIES (TAXATION) ACT 1987 - SECT 15DB When levy due for payment </name>
</b>** 

## SECT
<sect>   15DB. (1) Levy payable by a person on the lodgment of a particular return is due and payable on the day specified in a written notice given to the person by the Commissioner. 

<lf>   (2) The specified day must not be earlier than 21 days after the day on which the notice is given. </lf>
</sect>
**<b>

### <name>SUPERANNUATION ENTITIES (TAXATION) ACT 1987 - SECT 15DC Penalty for non-payment of levy </name>
</b>** 

## SECT
<sect>   15DC. (1) If any levy payable by a person in relation to a return for a year of income remains unpaid throughout the whole or a part of:<lf>   (a) the calendar month beginning at the start of the day after the day on which the levy became due and payable; or<lf>   (b) a later calendar month;<lf> the person is liable to pay to the Commonwealth, in respect of that calendar month, by way of penalty, the monthly amount worked out under subsection (2). However, this rule does not apply if the levy was fully paid before the 15th day of the calendar month referred to in paragraph (a).<lf> Note: Calendar month is defined by section 22 of the Acts Interpretation Act 1901\. 

<lf>   (2) The monthly amount for a calendar month is the amount obtained by calculating whichever is the greater of the following amounts:<lf>   (a) the amount calculated using the following formula and rounded up to the nearest whole dollar:<lf>                 Unpaid levy x 0.2<lf>                                12<lf> where:<lf>   Unpaid levy means the amount of levy unpaid in relation to the return as at the beginning of the calendar month;<lf>   (b) the amount calculated using the following formula and rounded up to the nearest whole dollar:<lf>             Minimum basic levy x 0.125<lf>                     amount<lf> where:<lf>   "Minimum basic levy amount" means the minimum basic levy amount for the year of income. <p><lf>   (3) In calculating, for the purposes of this section, the greater of 2 amounts, if those amounts are equal, the first-mentioned amount is taken to be greater than the other amount. <p><lf>   (4) Late payment penalty for a calendar month is due and payable at the end of the calendar month. </lf></p></lf></p></lf></lf></lf></lf></lf></lf></lf></lf></lf></lf></lf>
</lf></lf></lf></lf></sect>

### <name>SUPERANNUATION ENTITIES (TAXATION) ACT 1987 - SECT 15DD Recovery of levy etc. </name>
</b>** 

## SECT
<sect>   15DD. The following amounts may be recovered by the Commonwealth as debts due to the Commonwealth:<lf>   (a) levy that is due and payable;<lf>   (b) late payment penalty that is due and payable. </lf></lf></sect>
**<b>

### <name>SUPERANNUATION ENTITIES (TAXATION) ACT 1987 - SECT 15DE Payment of levy and late payment penalty </name>
</b>** 

## SECT
<sect>   15DE. Levy and late payment penalty are payable to the Commissioner. </sect>
**<b>

### <name>SUPERANNUATION ENTITIES (TAXATION) ACT 1987 - SECT 15DF Remission of levy and penalties </name>
</b>** 

## SECT
<sect>   15DF. The Commissioner may remit the whole or a part of the following:<lf>   (a) an amount of levy;<lf>   (b) an amount of late payment penalty. </lf></lf></sect>
**<b>

### <name>SUPERANNUATION ENTITIES (TAXATION) ACT 1987 - SECT 15DG Application of payments </name>
</b>**
