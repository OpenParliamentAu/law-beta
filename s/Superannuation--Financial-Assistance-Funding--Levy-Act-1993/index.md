###  Long Title

###  SUPERANNUATION (FINANCIAL ASSISTANCE FUNDING) LEVY ACT 1993- SECT 1  Short title [_see_ Note 1] 
<dl compact="">

		This Act may be cited as the _Superannuation (Financial Assistance Funding) Levy Act 1993_.

 </dl>
###  SUPERANNUATION (FINANCIAL ASSISTANCE FUNDING) LEVY ACT 1993- SECT 2  Commencement [_see_ Note 1] 
<dl compact="">

		This Act commences on the same day as Part 23 of the _Superannuation Industry (Supervision) Act 1993_.

 </dl>
###  SUPERANNUATION (FINANCIAL ASSISTANCE FUNDING) LEVY ACT 1993- SECT 3  Object of Act 
<dl compact="">

		The object of this Act is to impose levies on superannuation funds and

approved deposit funds for the purpose of funding financial assistance under

Part 23 of the _Superannuation Industry (Supervision) Act 1993_ to certain

funds that have suffered loss as a result of fraudulent conduct or theft.

 </dl>
###  SUPERANNUATION (FINANCIAL ASSISTANCE FUNDING) LEVY ACT 1993- SECT 4  Application of the Superannuation Industry (Supervision) Act 
<dl compact="">

		Section 9, and Division 2 of Part 1, of the _Superannuation Industry (Supervision)Act 1993_ apply in relation to this Act in a corresponding way

to the way in which they apply in relation to that Act.

 </dl>
###  SUPERANNUATION (FINANCIAL ASSISTANCE FUNDING) LEVY ACT 1993- SECT 5  Interpretation 
<dl compact="">

		In this Act, unless the contrary intention appears:

 </dl>
<dl compact=""><dl compact="">

_fund_ means a regulated superannuation fund or an approved deposit

fund, but does not include a self managed superannuation fund. _levy_ means levy imposed by regulations under section 6 of this Act.  </dl></dl>

**

###  SUPERANNUATION (FINANCIAL ASSISTANCE FUNDING) LEVY ACT 1993- SECT 6  Regulations may impose levies 
**

 <dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(1)</dt><dd>If the Minister makes a determination under Part 23 of the

_Superannuation Industry (Supervision) Act 1993_ to grant financial

assistance to a fund, the regulations may impose a levy or levies on each fund

other than:

</dd> </dl></dl>

<dl compact=""><dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(a)</dt><dd>the first-mentioned fund; or</dd>

<dt>(b)</dt><dd>another fund in respect of which such a determination was made in the same

financial year.

</dd>

</dl></dl></dl>

<center>Back to Top</center>

<dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(1A)</dt><dd>If the Minister makes more than one determination in a financial year

under Part&#160;23 of the _Superannuation Industry (Supervision) Act 1993_

to grant financial assistance, the regulations may impose a levy on each fund

that is not a fund in respect of which a determination was made in the same

financial year.</dd> <dt>(1B)</dt><dd>Regulations made for the purposes of subsection&#160;(1A) imposing a levy in respect of more than one determination must specify all the funds in respect of which determinations are made and the proportion of the levy that each fund represents.</dd> <dt>(2)</dt><dd>Each levy must be identified in the regulations by a unique number.</dd> <dt>(3)</dt><dd>Regulations imposing a levy in respect of one or more determinations may specify that either or both of the following are payable: </dd> </dl></dl>

<dl compact=""><dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(a)</dt><dd>a maximum amount of levy;</dd>

<dt>(b)</dt><dd>a minimum amount of levy.

</dd>

</dl></dl></dl>

**

###  SUPERANNUATION (FINANCIAL ASSISTANCE FUNDING) LEVY ACT 1993- SECT 7  Basis for prescribing amount of levy 
**

 <dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(1)</dt><dd>Subject to subsection&#160;(2):

</dd> </dl></dl>

<dl compact=""><dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(a)</dt><dd>the rate or rates of a levy that are imposed because of one or more grants

of financial assistance under Part&#160;23 of the _Superannuation Industry (Supervision)Act 1993_; and</dd>

<dt>(b)</dt><dd>the maximum amount of levy, and the minimum amount of levy, that is

payable;

</dd>

</dl></dl></dl>

are to be fixed on the basis that the amount of revenue to be raised by the levy or by the levy and any previous levy imposed because of the same grant or grants of financial assistance is not to exceed the amount of levy needed to recoup the Commonwealth for the amount of that financial assistance.

<dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(2)</dt><dd>The rate or rates of a levy imposed in a financial year are not to be such

that the total of the amounts payable in respect of the levy, or the total of

those amounts and any amounts payable in respect of a previous levy imposed in

the same financial year, would exceed 0.05% of the sum of the values at the end

of the previous financial year of all the assets of the funds on which the levy

or levies are imposed.</dd> <dt>(3)</dt><dd>In making regulations fixing the rate or rates of a levy, regard is to be had to subsections (1) and (2) but the validity of a regulation is not affected merely because it fixes a rate or rates of levy in excess of the maximum rate or rates required because of those subsections. </dd> </dl></dl>

**

###  SUPERANNUATION (FINANCIAL ASSISTANCE FUNDING) LEVY ACT 1993- SECT 8  Amount of levy 
**

 <dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(1)</dt><dd>The amount of a levy imposed on a fund is worked out using the formula:

</dd> </dl></dl>

<sub> ![A graphic exists here.](http://www.comlaw.gov.au/Details/C2004C00798/Html/068DFE3717CAC73ACA256F710050D7DB/$FILE/1202.gif)</sub>

<dl compact=""><dl compact="">

		where:

 </dl></dl>

<def><dl compact=""><dl compact="">

_Applicable rate_ means the rate (expressed as a decimal fraction)

that, under the regulations, is the rate of the levy applicable to the fund. _Value of assets_ means the value of the assets of the fund at the end of the last financial year of the fund before the day on which the regulation imposing the levy took effect.  </dl></dl>

<dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(1A)</dt><dd>However:

</dd> </dl></dl>

<dl compact=""><dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(a)</dt><dd>if there is a maximum amount of levy that is payable and the amount worked

out under subsection&#160;(1) is more than that maximum amount&#151;the amount of

levy imposed on the fund is the maximum amount; or</dd>

<dt>(b)</dt><dd>if there is a minimum amount of levy that is payable and the amount worked

out under subsection&#160;(1) is less than that minimum amount&#151;the amount of

levy imposed on the fund is the minimum amount.

</dd>

</dl></dl></dl>

<center>Back to Top</center>

<dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(2)</dt><dd>The applicable rate must not exceed 0.0005.

</dd> </dl></dl>

**

###  SUPERANNUATION (FINANCIAL ASSISTANCE FUNDING) LEVY ACT 1993- SECT 9  Levies may discriminate between classes of funds 
**

 <dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(1)</dt><dd>Regulations imposing a levy:

</dd> </dl></dl>

<dl compact=""><dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(a)</dt><dd>may provide different rates of the levy for different classes of funds;

but</dd>

<dt>(b)</dt><dd>must not otherwise discriminate between different funds.

</dd>

</dl></dl></dl>

<dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(2)</dt><dd>Regulations imposing a levy may define what constitutes a class of funds

for the purposes of the levy but must not define a class in such a way that the

levy would discriminate between States or parts of States.

</dd> </dl></dl>

**

###  SUPERANNUATION (FINANCIAL ASSISTANCE FUNDING) LEVY ACT 1993- SECT 10  Regulations

 </dl></dl>

<dl compact=""><dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(a)</dt><dd>required or permitted by this Act to be prescribed; or</dd>

<dt>(b)</dt><dd>necessary or convenient to be prescribed for carrying out or giving effect

to this Act.

</dd>

</dl></dl></dl>

<part>**Part 1&#151;Transitional provisions for validation of determinations relating to financial sector supervisory levies**</part>

**1  Definitions**

In this Part:
 **_Collection Act_** means the _Financial Institutions Supervisory Levies Collection Act 1998_.
 **_deferred payment day_** means the day that is 6 weeks after the day on which this Part commences.
 **_Imposition Act_** means any of the following Acts:

<dl compact=""><dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(a)</dt><dd>the _Authorised Non-operating Holding Companies Supervisory Levy Imposition Act 1998_; </dd>

<dt>(b)</dt><dd>the _General Insurance Supervisory Levy Imposition Act 1998_; </dd>

<dt>(c)</dt><dd>the _Life Insurance Supervisory Levy Imposition Act 1998_; </dd>

<dt>(d)</dt><dd>the _Retirement Savings Account Providers Supervisory Levy Imposition Act 1998_; </dd>

<dt>(e)</dt><dd>the _Superannuation Supervisory Levy Imposition Act 1998_.

</dd>

**_levy paying entity_** has the same meaning as in the Collection Act.
 **_Validation Act_** means any of the following Acts:

<dl compact=""><dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(a)</dt><dd>the _Authorised Non-operating Holding Companies Supervisory Levy Determination Validation Act 2000_;</dd>

<dt>(b)</dt><dd>the _General Insurance Supervisory Levy Determination Validation Act 2000_;</dd>

<dt>(c)</dt><dd>the _Life Insurance Supervisory Levy Determination Validation Act 2000_;</dd>

<dt>(d)</dt><dd>the _Retirement Savings Account Providers Supervisory Levy Determination Validation Act 2000_;</dd>

<dt>(e)</dt><dd>the _Superannuation Supervisory Levy Determination Validation Act 2000_.

</dd>

</dl></dl></dl>

**2  Deferral of date for paying levy**

(1)	This item applies to a levy paying entity if, because of section 4 of a Validation Act:

<dl compact=""><dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(a)</dt><dd>the entity is liable to pay levy imposed by an Imposition Act; and</dd>

<dt>(b)</dt><dd>the levy payable by the entity would, apart from this item, have been due

and payable under section 9 of the Collection Act before the deferred payment

day.

</dd>

</dl></dl></dl>

(2)	The levy payable by the entity is taken to be due and payable on the deferred payment day, despite section 9 of the Collection Act.

Note:	This provision affects the calculation of late payment penalty (if any) under section 10 of the Collection Act.

**3  No retrospective criminal liability**

Nothing in this Part or the Validation Acts is taken to make a person criminally liable in respect of acts or omissions of the person before the day on which this Part commences, if the person would not have been so liable had this Part and the Validation Acts not been enacted.
