###  Long Title 
**
###  STATES GRANTS (SCIENCE LABORATORIES) ACT 1971- SECT 1  Short title [_see_ Note 1] 
This Act may be cited as the _States Grants (Science Laboratories) Act 1971_.

 
###  STATES GRANTS (SCIENCE LABORATORIES) ACT 1971- SECT 2  Interpretation 
In this Act, unless the contrary intention appears:

 
<dl compact=""><dl compact="">

_school_ means a school or a similar institution, whether conducted

by a State or not. _the period to which this Act applies_ means the period commencing on the first day of July, One thousand nine hundred and seventy-one, and ending on the thirtieth day of June, One thousand nine hundred and seventy-five.  </dl></dl>

**

###  STATES GRANTS (SCIENCE LABORATORIES) ACT 1971- SECT 3  Grants for science laboratories and equipment  
**

 <dl compact="">

<dt>(1)</dt><dd>The Minister may authorize the payment to a State, under this Act, during

the period to which this Act applies or, subject to the next succeeding

sub-section, after that period, by way of financial assistance, of such amounts

as, subject to this section, he determines.</dd> <dt>(2)</dt><dd>The Minister shall not authorize the payment of an amount to a State under this section after the period to which this Act applies unless he is satisfied that the amount will be applied for the purpose of meeting commitments undertaken during that period.</dd> <dt>(3)</dt><dd>The Minister shall not authorize payments to a State under this section that: </dd> </dl>

<dl compact=""><dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(a)</dt><dd>exceed, in the aggregate, the amount specified in the sixth column of the

Schedule to this Act opposite to the name of the State;</dd>

<dt>(b)</dt><dd>in the case of payments authorized on or before the thirtieth day of June,

One thousand nine hundred and seventy-two&#151;exceed, in the aggregate,

one-quarter of that amount;</dd>

<dt>(c)</dt><dd>in the case of payments authorized on or before the thirtieth day of June,

One thousand nine hundred and seventy-three&#151;exceed, in the aggregate, one-half

of that amount; or</dd>

<dt>(d)</dt><dd>in the case of payments authorized on or before the thirtieth day of June,

One thousand nine hundred and seventy-four&#151;exceed, in the aggregate,

three-quarters of that amount.

</dd>

</dl></dl></dl>

<dl compact="">

<dt>(4)</dt><dd>Payment of an amount to a State under this Act is subject to the condition

that the amount will be applied by the State, as approved by the Minister, for

purposes in connexion with laboratories and equipment for use in the teaching

of science in schools at the secondary level of education.</dd> <dt>(5)</dt><dd>The Minister shall not grant an approval under the last preceding subsection for the application of an amount by a State in respect of a school where: </dd> </dl>

<dl compact=""><dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(a)</dt><dd>in the case of a school conducted by the State-if the approval were

granted, the total of the amounts approved in respect of schools conducted by

the State would exceed the amount specified in the second column of the

Schedule to this Act opposite to the name of the State;</dd>

<dt>(b)</dt><dd>in the case of a school not conducted by the State, being a Roman Catholic

school&#151;if the approval were granted, the total of the amounts approved in

respect of schools in the State not conducted by the State, being Roman

Catholic schools, would exceed the amount specified in the third column of the

Schedule to this Act opposite to the name of the State; or</dd>

<dt>(c)</dt><dd>in the case of a school not conducted by the State, not being a Roman

Catholic school&#151;if the approval were granted, the total of the amounts

approved in respect of schools in the State not conducted by the State, not

being Roman Catholic schools, would exceed the amount specified in the fourth

column of the Schedule to this Act opposite to the name of the State.

</dd>

</dl></dl></dl>

###  STATES GRANTS (SCIENCE LABORATORIES) ACT 1971- SECT 4  Regulations may vary amounts  
**

 <dl compact="">

		At any time during the period to which this Act applies, provision may be

made by the regulations that this Act shall have effect as if the amounts

specified in the third or fourth column, or both of those columns, and the

amounts specified in the fifth and sixth columns, of the Schedule to this Act

opposite to the names of any States were varied in accordance with the

regulations, but not so as to vary the total of the amounts specified in the

fifth column, or the total of the amounts specified in the sixth column, of

that Schedule.

 </dl>

**

###  STATES GRANTS (SCIENCE LABORATORIES) ACT 1971- SECT 5  Annual statement by Minister  
**

 <dl compact="">

<dt>(1)</dt><dd>The Minister shall cause a statement to be laid before each House of the

Parliament as soon as practicable after each thirtieth day of June during the

period to which this Act applies describing the arrangements in accordance with

which payments under this Act have been authorized in the year that ended on

that day and stating, in respect of each State:

</dd> </dl>

<dl compact=""><dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(a)</dt><dd>in relation to schools not being schools conducted by the State:

</dd>

</dl></dl></dl>

<dl compact=""><dl compact=""><dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(i)</dt><dd>the total of the amounts paid to the State under this Act in that year;

and</dd>

<dt>(ii)</dt><dd>the names of the schools in respect of which approvals for the

application of moneys included in that total have been given, and the amount

approved for expenditure in respect of each such school; and

</dd>

</dl></dl></dl></dl>

<dl compact=""><dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(b)</dt><dd>in relation to schools conducted by the State:

</dd>

</dl></dl></dl>

<dl compact=""><dl compact=""><dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(i)</dt><dd>the total of the amounts paid to the State under this Act in that year;

and</dd>

<dt>(ii)</dt><dd>the names of the schools in respect of which the State has, during that

year, applied amounts paid to the State under this Act.

</dd>

</dl></dl></dl></dl>

<dl compact="">

<dt>(2)</dt><dd>For the purposes of the last preceding subsection, any payment made to a

State under this Act after the period to which this Act applies shall be deemed

to have been made during the year that ended on the thirtieth day of June, One

thousand nine hundred and seventy-five.

</dd> </dl>

**

###  STATES GRANTS (SCIENCE LABORATORIES) ACT 1971- SECT 6  Appropriation  
**

 <dl compact="">

		Payments under this Act shall be made out of the Consolidated Revenue Fund,

which is appropriated accordingly.

 </dl>

**

###  STATES GRANTS (SCIENCE LABORATORIES) ACT 1971- SECT 7  Regulations  
**

 <dl compact="">

		The Governor-General may make regulations for the purposes of section&#160;4

of this Act, including regulations repealing or amending regulations previously

made under this section.

 </dl>
