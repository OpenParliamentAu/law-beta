**

###  FRINGE BENEFITS TAX (APPLICATION TO THE COMMONWEALTH) ACT 1986 
**


**

###  Compilation Information 
**










**

###  Long Title 
**

###  FRINGE BENEFITS TAX (APPLICATION TO THE COMMONWEALTH) ACT 1986- SECT 1  Short title [_see_ Note 1] 
<dl compact="">

		This Act may be cited as the _Fringe Benefits Tax (Application to the Commonwealth) Act 1986_.

 </dl>
###  FRINGE BENEFITS TAX (APPLICATION TO THE COMMONWEALTH) ACT 1986- SECT 2  Commencement [_see_ Note 1] 
<dl compact="">

		This Act shall come into operation on the day on which the _Fringe Benefits Tax Assessment Act 1986_ comes into operation.

 </dl>
###  FRINGE BENEFITS TAX (APPLICATION TO THE COMMONWEALTH) ACT 1986- SECT 2A  Objects 
<dl compact="">

		The objects of this Act are to provide for:

 </dl>
<dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(a)</dt><dd>the notional application of fringe benefits tax in relation to benefits

provided in respect of the employment of Commonwealth employees; and</dd>

<dt>(b)</dt><dd>the calculation of the reportable fringe benefits totals of those

employees; and</dd>

<dt>(c)</dt><dd>the calculation of the reportable fringe benefits amounts of those

employees in respect of that employment.

</dd>

</dl></dl>
###  FRINGE BENEFITS TAX (APPLICATION TO THE COMMONWEALTH) ACT 1986- SECT 3  Interpretation  
<dl compact="">

<dt>(1)</dt><dd>In this Act, unless the contrary intention appears:

</dd> </dl>
<dl compact=""><dl compact="">

_Assessment Act_ means the _Fringe Benefits Tax Assessment Act 1986_. _Commonwealth employee_ means an employee of the Commonwealth. _Department_ means:  </dl></dl>

<dl compact=""><dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(a)</dt><dd>a Department of State;</dd>

<dt>(b)</dt><dd>a Department of the Parliament; or</dd>

<dt>(c)</dt><dd>an Executive Agency or Statutory Agency (within the meaning of the

_Public Service Act 1999_).

</dd>

</dl></dl></dl>

<def><dl compact=""><dl compact="">

_Entitlements Act_ means the _Veteran&apos;s Entitlements Act 1986._ _responsible Department_, in relation to the employment of a Commonwealth employee, means:  </dl></dl>

<center>Back to Top</center>

<dl compact=""><dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(a)</dt><dd>where the remuneration in respect of that employment is or was paid wholly

or principally out of money appropriated under an annual Appropriation Act&#151;the

Department in respect of which the money was appropriated;</dd>

<dt>(b)</dt><dd>where the remuneration in respect of that employment is or was paid wholly

or principally out of money appropriated under an Act other than an annual

Appropriation Act:

</dd>

</dl></dl></dl>

<dl compact=""><dl compact=""><dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(i)</dt><dd>if the employee performs or performed the duties of that employment in, or

in respect of, a Department&#151;that Department; or</dd>

<dt>(ii)</dt><dd>in any other case&#151;the Department of State administered by the Minister

who administers the Act under which that money was appropriated, insofar as the

Act appropriated that money; and

</dd>

</dl></dl></dl></dl>

<dl compact=""><dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(c)</dt><dd>where the remuneration in respect of that employment is or was paid wholly

or principally out of money appropriated by the Constitution&#151;the Department of

the Special Minister of State.

</dd>

</dl></dl></dl>

<dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(2)</dt><dd>Unless the contrary intention appears, an expression that is used in this

Act and in the Assessment Act has the same meaning in this Act as it has in the

Assessment Act.

</dd> </dl></dl>

**

###  FRINGE BENEFITS TAX (APPLICATION TO THE COMMONWEALTH) ACT 1986- SECT 4  Application of Assessment Act in relation to Commonwealth employment 
**

<dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(1)</dt><dd>Subject to this Act and to such modifications as are prescribed, the

Assessment Act applies, in respect of any matter or thing in respect of the

employment of a Commonwealth employee, as if:

</dd> </dl></dl>

<dl compact=""><dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(a)</dt><dd>the employee were employed by the responsible Department and not by the

Commonwealth;</dd>

<dt>(b)</dt><dd>the responsible Department were a company and each other Department, and

each authority of the Commonwealth, were a company related to the responsible

Department; and</dd>

<dt>(c)</dt><dd>the responsible Department were a government body.

</dd>

</dl></dl></dl>

<dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(2)</dt><dd>In subsection (1), _modifications_ includes additions,

omissions and substitutions.

</dd> </dl></dl>

**

###  FRINGE BENEFITS TAX (APPLICATION TO THE COMMONWEALTH) ACT 1986- SECT 5  Application of Act to certain Commonwealth authorities 
**

 <dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(1)</dt><dd>Subject to such modifications as are prescribed, this Act applies in

relation to an authority of the Commonwealth referred to in paragraph (e) of

the definition of _employer_ in subsection&#160;136(1) of the

Assessment Act in like manner as it applies in relation to a Department.</dd> <dt>(2)</dt><dd>In subsection (1), _modifications_ includes additions, omissions and substitutions. </dd> </dl></dl>

**

###  FRINGE BENEFITS TAX (APPLICATION TO THE COMMONWEALTH) ACT 1986- SECT 5A  Modified objection, review and appeal provisions 
**

 <dl compact=""><dl compact="">

		If, apart from this section, Part IVC of the _Taxation Administration Act 1953_ would apply to an objection made under the Assessment Act in its

application in accordance with this Act, then that Part applies as if

subsection 14ZX (4), section 14ZZ and Divisions 4 and 5 of that Part were

omitted.

 </dl></dl>

**

###  FRINGE BENEFITS TAX (APPLICATION TO THE COMMONWEALTH) ACT 1986- SECT 6  Exemption of certain benefits provided under the Defence Service Homes Act  
**

<dl compact=""><dl compact="">

		For the purposes of the application of the Assessment Act in accordance with

this Act, where a benefit is provided under the _Defence Service Homes Act 1918_ in respect of the employment of a Commonwealth employee by reason of

the employee being an eligible person within the meaning of that Act otherwise

than by virtue of paragraph (h) or (i) of the definition of _Australian Soldier_ in subsection 4(1) of that Act, the benefit is an exempt benefit.

 </dl></dl>

<center>Back to Top</center>

**

###  FRINGE BENEFITS TAX (APPLICATION TO THE COMMONWEALTH) ACT 1986- SECT 6AA  Exemption of benefits provided to certain people 
**

 <dl compact=""><dl compact="">

		For the purposes of the application of the Assessment Act in accordance with

this Act, if a benefit (other than a benefit referred to in section 6) is

provided, in respect of the employment of a Commonwealth employee, because of

the employee being:

 </dl></dl>

<dl compact=""><dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(a)</dt><dd>a veteran as defined by subsection 5C(1) of the Entitlements Act to whom,

or in respect of whom, a pension is payable under Part II of that Act;  or</dd>

<dt>(b)</dt><dd>a veteran as defined by subsection 5C(1) of the Entitlements Act who is

eligible to receive a service pension, or in respect of whom a service pension

is payable, under Part III of that Act; or</dd>

<dt>(c)</dt><dd>a member of the Forces, or a member of a Peacekeeping Force, as defined by

subsection 68(1) of the Entitlements Act to whom, or in respect of whom,

pension is payable under Part IV of that Act; or</dd>

<dt>(d)</dt><dd>a person who has received a lump sum under Part&#160;2 of Chapter&#160;4

of the _Military Rehabilitation and Compensation Act 2004_ or who is

receiving payments under that Part; or</dd>

<dt>(e)</dt><dd>a person who is receiving a Special Rate Disability Pension under

Part&#160;6 of Chapter&#160;4 of the _Military Rehabilitation and Compensation Act 2004_;

</dd>

</dl></dl></dl>

the benefit is an exempt benefit.   

**

###  FRINGE BENEFITS TAX (APPLICATION TO THE COMMONWEALTH) ACT 1986- SECT 6AB  Exemption of benefits provided to Commonwealth employees under the Entitlements Act and the Military Rehabilitation and Compensation Act 
**

<dl compact=""><dl compact="">

		For the purposes of the application of the Assessment Act in accordance with

this Act, if a benefit is provided under the Entitlements Act or the

_Military Rehabilitation and Compensation Act 2004_, in respect of the

employment of a Commonwealth employee, the benefit is an exempt  benefit.  

 </dl></dl>

**

###  FRINGE BENEFITS TAX (APPLICATION TO THE COMMONWEALTH) ACT 1986- SECT 6AC  Exemption of health care benefits provided to certain members of the Defence Force 
**

<dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(1)</dt><dd>For the purposes of the application of the Assessment Act in accordance

with this Act, if a benefit that consists of the provision of health care and

is not otherwise an exempt benefit is provided, in respect of the employment of

a Commonwealth employee, because of the employee being a member of the Defence

Force, the first-mentioned benefit is an exempt benefit.  </dd> <dt>(2)</dt><dd>In this section, a reference to an employee being a member of the  Defence Force is a reference to an employee who is, was formerly, or will become, a member of the Defence Force.   </dd> </dl></dl>

**

###  FRINGE BENEFITS TAX (APPLICATION TO THE COMMONWEALTH) ACT 1986- SECT 6A  No entitlement to rebate of tax 
**

 <dl compact=""><dl compact="">

		For the purposes of the application of the Assessment Act in accordance  with

this Act, it is to be assumed that section 65J of that Act (which deals with

rebates) had not been enacted.  

 </dl></dl>

**

###  FRINGE BENEFITS TAX (APPLICATION TO THE COMMONWEALTH) ACT 1986- SECT 7  Directions by Minister for Finance 
**

 <dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(1)</dt><dd>The Minister for Finance may give such directions in writing as are

necessary or convenient to be given for carrying out or giving effect to this

Act and, in particular, may give directions in relation to the transfer of

money within the Public Account.</dd> <dt>(2)</dt><dd>Directions under subsection (1) have effect, and shall be complied with, notwithstanding any other law of the Commonwealth. </dd> </dl></dl>

**

###  FRINGE BENEFITS TAX (APPLICATION TO THE COMMONWEALTH) ACT 1986- SECT 8   Annual report 
**

 <dl compact=""><dl compact="">

		The report by the Commissioner under section 4 of the Assessment Act shall

include a report on the working of this Act, including any breaches or evasions

of this Act of which the Commissioner has notice.

 </dl></dl>

<center>Back to Top</center>

**

###  FRINGE BENEFITS TAX (APPLICATION TO THE COMMONWEALTH) ACT 1986- SECT 9  Regulations 
**

 <dl compact=""><dl compact="">

		The Governor-General may make regulations, not inconsistent with this Act,

prescribing matters:

 </dl></dl>

<dl compact=""><dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(a)</dt><dd>required or permitted by this Act to be prescribed; or</dd>

<dt>(b)</dt><dd>necessary or convenient to be prescribed for carrying out or giving effect

to this Act.

</dd>

</dl></dl></dl>
