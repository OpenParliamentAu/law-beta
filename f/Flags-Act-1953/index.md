### <name>FLAGS ACT 1953 - LONG TITLE </name>
</b>** 

## SECT
<sect>       An Act to declare a certain Flag to be the Australian National<lf>           Flag and make to other provision with respect to Flags<lf> </lf></lf></sect>
**<b>

### <name>FLAGS ACT 1953 - SECT 1 Short title </name>
</b>** 

## SECT
<sect>   1\. This Act may be cited as the Flags Act 1953.*1* SEE NOTES TO FIRST ARTICLE OF THIS CHAPTER . </sect>
**<b>

### <name>FLAGS ACT 1953 - SECT 2 Extension to Territories </name>
</b>** 

## SECT
<sect>   2\. This Act extends to all the Territories. </sect>
**<b>

### <name>FLAGS ACT 1953 - SECT 3 The Australian National Flag </name>
</b>** 

## SECT
<sect>   3\. The blue flag described in Schedule 1, being the flag a reproduction of which is set out in Part I of Schedule 2, is declared to be the Australian National Flag. </sect>
**<b>

### <name>FLAGS ACT 1953 - SECT 4 The Australian Red Ensign </name>
</b>** 

## SECT
<sect>   4\. The red flag described in Schedule 1, being the flag a reproduction of which is set out in Part II of Schedule 2, shall be known as the Australian Red Ensign. </sect>
**<b>

### <name>FLAGS ACT 1953 - SECT 5 Other flags </name>
</b>** 

## SECT
<sect>   5\. The Governor-General may, by Proclamation, appoint such other flags and ensigns of Australia as he thinks fit. </sect>
**<b>

### <name>FLAGS ACT 1953 - SECT 6 Warrants to use flags </name>
</b>** 

## SECT
<sect>   6\. The Governor-General may, by warrant, authorize a person, body or authority to use a flag or ensign referred to in, or appointed under, this Act, either without defacement or defaced in the manner specified in the warrant. </sect>
**<b>

### <name>FLAGS ACT 1953 - SECT 7 Rules as to use of flags </name>
</b>** 

## SECT
<sect>   7\. The Governor-General may make, and cause to be published, rules for the guidance of persons in connexion with the flying or use of flags or ensigns referred to in, or appointed under, this Act. </sect>
**<b>

### <name>FLAGS ACT 1953 - SECT 8 Flying of Union Jack </name>
</b>** 

## SECT
<sect>   8\. This Act does not affect the right or privilege of a person to fly the Union Jack. </sect>
