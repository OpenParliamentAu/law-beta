###  Long Title 
**

###  FINANCIAL AGREEMENT ACT 1994- SECT 1  Short title [_see_ Note 1] 
This Act may be cited as the _Financial Agreement Act 1994_.

 
###  FINANCIAL AGREEMENT ACT 1994- SECT 2  Commencement [_see_ Note 1] 
<dt>(1)</dt><dd>Subject to subsection&#160;(2), this Act commences on a day to be fixed by

Proclamation.</dd> <dt>(2)</dt><dd>If the commencement of this Act is not fixed by a Proclamation published in the _Gazette_ within the period of 12 months beginning on the day on which this Act receives the Royal Assent, this Act is repealed on the first day after the end of that period. </dd> 
###  FINANCIAL AGREEMENT ACT 1994- SECT 3  Interpretation 
In this Act:

 
<dl compact=""><dl compact="">

_1994 Financial Agreement_ means the agreement approved under

section&#160;4 as amended by any subsequent agreement approved by the Parliament.

 </dl></dl>

**

###  FINANCIAL AGREEMENT ACT 1994- SECT 4  Approval of agreement 
**

 <dl compact="">

		The agreement (a copy of which is set out in the Schedule) is approved.

 </dl>

<dl compact=""><dl compact="">

Note:	The 1994 Financial Agreement rescinds the agreement and

arrangements specified in clause&#160;16 of the 1994 Financial Agreement to the

extent provided in that clause.

 </dl></dl>

**

###  FINANCIAL AGREEMENT ACT 1994- SECT 5  Appropriation 
**

 <dl compact="">

		The Consolidated Revenue Fund is appropriated to the extent necessary for the

purpose of carrying out the 1994 Financial Agreement on the part of the

Commonwealth.

 </dl>

**

###  FINANCIAL AGREEMENT ACT 1994- SECT 6  Establishment of trust account 
**

 <dl compact="">

<dt>(1)</dt><dd>A trust account to be known as the Debt Retirement Reserve Trust Account

is established.</dd> <dt>(2)</dt><dd>The account is a component of the Reserved Money Fund.</dd> <dt>(3)</dt><dd>The purpose of the account is for the payment and receipt of moneys in accordance with the 1994 Financial Agreement.</dd> <dt>(4)</dt><dd>There must be transferred to the account from the Consolidated Revenue Fund amounts equal to all money received by the Commonwealth under the 1994 Financial Agreement. </dd> </dl>

<center>Back to Top</center>

**

###  FINANCIAL AGREEMENT ACT 1994- SECT 7  Establishment of State and Northern Territory accounts 
**

 <dl compact="">

		A separate account in respect of each State and the Northern Territory is

established in the Debt Retirement Reserve Trust Account.

 </dl>

**

###  FINANCIAL AGREEMENT ACT 1994- SECT 8  Transfer of moneys into the Northern Territory account 
**

 <dl compact="">

		As soon as practicable after the commencement of this Act, the Treasurer must

pay the total amount of money in the Northern Territory Debt Sinking Fund into

the Territory's account in the Debt Retirement Reserve Trust Account.

 </dl>

<dl compact=""><dl compact="">

Note:	The Northern Territory Debt Sinking Fund was established

under section&#160;62A of the _Audit Act 1901_. Similar provision for the

transfer of the balance of the State's accounts in the National Debt Sinking

Fund is made by section&#160;7 of the _National Debt Sinking Fund Repeal Act 1994_.

 </dl></dl>
