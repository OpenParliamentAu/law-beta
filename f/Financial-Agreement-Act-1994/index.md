**<b>

### <name>FINANCIAL AGREEMENT ACT 1994 - LONG TITLE </name>
</b>** 

## SECT
<sect>       An Act to approve an agreement between the Commonwealth, the<lf>          States and the Territories, and for related purposes<lf> </lf></lf></sect>
**<b>

### <name>FINANCIAL AGREEMENT ACT 1994 - SECT 1 Short title </name>
</b>** 

## SECT
<sect>   1\. This Act may be cited as the Financial Agreement Act 1994\. 

(Minister's second reading speech made in -<lf>           House of Representatives on 10 May 1994<lf>           Senate on 12 May 1994)<lf> </lf></lf></lf>
</sect>
**<b>

### <name>FINANCIAL AGREEMENT ACT 1994 - SECT 2 Commencement </name>
</b>** 

## SECT
<sect>   2.(1) Subject to subsection (2), this Act commences on a day to be fixed by Proclamation. 

  (2) If the commencement of this Act is not fixed by a Proclamation published in the Gazette within the period of 12 months beginning on the day on which this Act receives the Royal Assent, this Act is repealed on the first day after the end of that period. 
</sect>
**<b>

### <name>FINANCIAL AGREEMENT ACT 1994 - SECT 3 Interpretation </name>
</b>** 

## SECT
<sect>   3\. In this Act:<lf>   "1994 Financial Agreement" means the agreement approved under section 4 as amended by any subsequent agreement approved by the Parliament. </lf></sect>
**<b>

### <name>FINANCIAL AGREEMENT ACT 1994 - SECT 4 Approval of agreement </name>
</b>** 

## SECT
<sect>   4\. The agreement (a copy of which is set out in the Schedule) is approved.<lf> Note: The 1994 Financial Agreement rescinds the agreement and arrangements specified in clause 16 of the 1994 Financial Agreement to the extent provided in that clause. </lf></sect>
**<b>

### <name>FINANCIAL AGREEMENT ACT 1994 - SECT 5 Appropriation </name>
</b>** 

## SECT
<sect>   5\. The Consolidated Revenue Fund is appropriated to the extent necessary for the purpose of carrying out the 1994 Financial Agreement on the part of the Commonwealth. </sect>
**<b>

### <name>FINANCIAL AGREEMENT ACT 1994 - SECT 6 Establishment of trust account </name>
</b>** 

## SECT
<sect>   6.(1) A trust account to be known as the Debt Retirement Reserve Trust Account is established. 

  (2) The account is a trust account for the purposes of section 62A of the Audit Act 1901\. 

  (3) The purpose of the account is for the payment and receipt of moneys in accordance with the 1994 Financial Agreement. 
</sect>
**<b>

### <name>FINANCIAL AGREEMENT ACT 1994 - SECT 7 Establishment of State and Northern Territory accounts </name>
</b>** 

## SECT
<sect>   7\. A separate account in respect of each State and the Northern Territory is established in the Debt Retirement Reserve Trust Account. </sect>
**<b>

### <name>FINANCIAL AGREEMENT ACT 1994 - SECT 8 Transfer of moneys into the Northern Territory account </name>
</b>** 

## SECT
<sect>   8\. As soon as practicable after the commencement of this Act, the Treasurer must pay the total amount of money in the Northern Territory Debt Sinking Fund into the Territory's account in the Debt Retirement Reserve Trust Account.<lf> Note: The Northern Territory Debt Sinking Fund was established under section 62A of the Audit Act 1901\. Similar provision for the transfer of the balance of the State's accounts in the National Debt Sinking Fund is made by section 7 of the National Debt Sinking Fund Repeal Act 1994\. </lf></sect>
