<dl compact=""><dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(a)</dt><dd>the Territory of Christmas Island; or</dd>

<dt>(b)</dt><dd>the Territory of Cocos (Keeling) Islands.

</dd>

</dl></dl></dl>

<def><dl compact=""><dl compact="">

_installation in the Joint Petroleum Development Area_ means a

resources installation, or a sea installation, that is attached to the seabed

in the Joint Petroleum Development Area. _Joint Petroleum Development Area_ has the same meaning as in the _Petroleum (Timor Sea Treaty) Act 2003_. _resources installation_ has the same meaning as in subsection 4(1) of the _Customs Act 1901_. _sea installation_ has the same meaning as in subsection 4(1) of the _Customs Act 1901_.  </dl></dl>

###  PASSENGER MOVEMENT CHARGE ACT 1978- SECT 4  Travel involving intermediate destinations 
**

 <dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(1)</dt><dd>For the purposes of this Act, if:

</dd> </dl></dl>

<dl compact=""><dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(a)</dt><dd>a person departs from Australia for an external Territory; and</dd>

<dt>(b)</dt><dd>the person is not ordinarily resident in that external Territory; and</dd>

<dt>(c)</dt><dd>the person intends, at the time of the departure, to depart from that

external Territory for another country, or for an installation in the Joint

Petroleum Development Area, within 3 months after the departure from Australia;

</dd>

</dl></dl></dl>

<center>Back to Top</center>

the first-mentioned departure is taken to be a departure of the person from Australia for that other country or for that installation, as the case requires.

<dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(2)</dt><dd>For the purposes of this Act (including subsection (1)), if:

</dd> </dl></dl>

<dl compact=""><dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(a)</dt><dd>a person departs from Australia for another country or for an installation

in the Joint Petroleum Development Area; and</dd>

<dt>(b)</dt><dd>the person intends, at the time of the departure, to depart from that

other country, or from an installation in the Joint Petroleum Development Area,

for an external Territory within 7 days after the departure from Australia;

</dd>

</dl></dl></dl>

the first-mentioned departure is taken to be a departure from Australia for that external Territory.

<dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(3)</dt><dd>For the purposes of this Act, if:

</dd> </dl></dl>

<dl compact=""><dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(a)</dt><dd>a person departs from an Indian Ocean Territory for another country or for

an installation in the Joint Petroleum Development Area; and</dd>

<dt>(b)</dt><dd>the person intends, at the time of the departure, to depart from that

other country, or from an installation in the Joint Petroleum Development Area,

for a place in Australia other than that first-mentioned Indian Ocean Territory

within 7 days after the first-mentioned departure;

</dd>

</dl></dl></dl>

the first-mentioned departure is taken not to be a departure from Australia for another country or for an installation in the Joint Petroleum Development Area.

<dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(4)</dt><dd>For the purposes of this Act, if:

</dd> </dl></dl>

<dl compact=""><dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(a)</dt><dd>a person departs from a part of Australia other than an Indian Ocean

Territory for another country or for an installation in the Joint Petroleum

Development Area; and</dd>

<dt>(b)</dt><dd>the person intends, at the time of the departure, to depart from that

other country, or from an installation in the Joint Petroleum Development Area,

for an Indian Ocean Territory within 7 days after the first-mentioned departure;

</dd>

</dl></dl></dl>

the first-mentioned departure is taken not to be a departure from Australia for another country or for an installation in the Joint Petroleum Development Area. 

**

###  PASSENGER MOVEMENT CHARGE ACT 1978- SECT 5  Imposition of passenger movement charge 
**

 <dl compact=""><dl compact="">

		Charge, called passenger movement charge, is imposed in respect of the

departure of a person from Australia:

 </dl></dl>

<dl compact=""><dl compact=""><dl compact="">

<dt>(a)</dt><dd>for another country, after the commencement of this Act; or</dd>

<dt>(b)</dt><dd>for an installation in the Joint Petroleum Development Area, on or after 1

July 1995;

</dd>

</dl></dl></dl>

whether or not the person intends to return to Australia. 

**

###  PASSENGER MOVEMENT CHARGE ACT 1978- SECT 6  Rate of passenger movement charge 
**

 <dl compact=""><dl compact="">

		The rate of charge imposed by this Act in respect of the departure of a

person from Australia is $38.

 </dl></dl>
